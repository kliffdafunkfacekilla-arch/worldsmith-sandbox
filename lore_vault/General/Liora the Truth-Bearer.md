## The Defection
[[Magistar Virantor]]’s top propagandist. She was the voice that convinced the masses that slavery was "divine service". One day, she looked at the faces of the people she was lying to and simply spoke the truth into the magical amplifiers. The resulting riot nearly tore the capital apart, giving the gods the opening they needed.
