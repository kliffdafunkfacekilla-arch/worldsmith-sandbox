### Master Lore Bible: The Guerrilla Clans (Ghostwind Raiders)

##### 1. The Ghost-Link Mandate: Origins and Affiliation

**Strategic Intent**  The Ghostwind Raiders are classified as "Ghost-Link" assets—a clandestine, deniable fleet existing outside the public manifest of the  **Meridian Chain**  (The Ghost-Light Cartel). Orchestrated by the Hexarchy under the covert oversight of  **Grand-Link Nero** , these assets execute high-risk kinetics where the  **Order of the Wick**  requires maximum plausible deniability. Operational parameters dictate 100% deniability for all kinetics involving  **Dragonstone**  retrieval, asset denial, and intelligence harvesting within  **Sacred Grove**  proximity.**The "Mutual Profit" Paradox**  The Raiders do not operate as mere scavengers; they are the primary stabilizers for the  **Abyssal Resource Chains** . By destabilizing rival trade routes and hitting "Sacred Groves" to secure  **Abyssal Loot** , they feed the  **Royal Whim Markets**  while ensuring the Hexarchy maintains a monopoly on high-yield Aetheric commodities. Their mandate is distilled into three tactical priorities:

**Surgical Extraction** : Deployment into "Chaos-Free Corridors" to seize  **Aether-Tech**  or  **Dragonstone**  shards before rival cartels can stabilize the local reality.

**Systemic Sabotage** : Destruction of rival infrastructure in "Bridge-Cities," utilizing  **Aetheric Tension**  shifts to mask sabotage as natural "Meanders."

**Groves Reconnaissance** : Mapping the "Signature Chaos" of imprisoned  **Magistars**  to provide the  **Order of the Wick**  with data for future containment adjustments.**Operational Anonymity**  The Hexarchy maintains the "Black Ledger" of shadow-funding. Publicly,  **Grand-Link Nero**  denounces the Raiders as lawless predators. Sub-rosa, the Cartel provides  **Chitin-Hull**  airframes and access to  **Ghost-Light Towers** . This infrastructure allows Raiders to navigate "Chaos-Free Corridors" during peak atmospheric instability while officially remaining non-entities.

##### 2. The 'Unseen Hand' Doctrine

**Foundational Philosophy**  The "Unseen Hand" rejects the blunt-force attrition of the  **Iron Caldera**  in favor of surgical precision. It utilizes the  *Scute*  "Code of Vengeance"—retribution focused solely on the offender—and the  *Wolverine*  "Art of Misdirection" to bypass defenses. The goal is the removal of specific variables without alerting the wider system.**Tactical Pillars**| Pillar | Operational Methodology || ------ | ------ || **Infiltration** | Leveraging specialized physiology and  **Aetheric Tension**  to bypass "Life-Sense" wards and thermal surveillance. || **Extraction** | Prioritizing high-yield intelligence and  **Dragonstone**  density data over bulk bullion, focused on the  **Abyssal Resource Chain** . || **Phased Withdrawal** | Deployment of decoy signatures and "Zen-stasis" maneuvering to vanish before the local "Meander" pivots. |

**The Silence of the Void**  Raider vessels utilize  **Bismuth**  logic-nullifiers to leverage the properties of the  **Geometric Void** . According to the  **Twelve Elemental Conductors**  protocol,  **Bismuth**  stops  **Arcane**  energy, effectively lobotomizing bureaucratic surveillance nets.  *Note: Archivists warn that Bismuth is bypassed by Spirit energy; entities or wards powered by the School of Phosphorus remain the sole technical blind spot in Raider invisibility.*

##### 3. Aether-Tech Armament: Pop-Bolts and Leap-Charms

**The D-Dust Infusion**  Manufacturing requires "Dusted-ink"—a high-risk fusion of  **Shadow-Seal Ink**  and pulverized  **Dragonstone**  ( **D-Dust** ). Before application, the "Pre-Ink Council" (modeled after  **Avian Shadow-Set**  rituals) is convened to synchronize the creator's intent with the weapon's discharge. Failure in synchronization results in a "Fractured Intent"  **Blowout** .**Dusted-ink Pop-Bolts**  These projectiles utilize precisely tuned metal coils to trigger localized, reality-warping "Blowouts" upon impact.| Metal Conductor | Elemental Interaction & Chaos Effect || ------ | ------ || **Silicon** | Stops  **Shock** ; shatters into high-frequency  **Sonic**  blasts for hull-breaching. || **Lead** | Stops  **Sonic** ; dents into massive  **Kinetic Force**  to crush armored targets. || **Gold** | Stops  **Acid** ; conducts  **Shock** , ideal for boarding  **Sump-Kin**  vessels or frying electronics. || **Tungsten** | Stops  **Fire** ; flash-freezes the target via  **Cold**  interaction (Stasis-lock). |

**Leap-Charms**  Single-use trinkets utilizing "Aetheric Tension" for instantaneous repositioning.

**Tungsten Coils** : Flash-freezes the air molecules beneath the user to create a temporary, solid foothold in mid-air.

**Titanium Components** : Utilizes the property where  **Titanium**  stops  **Cold**  and heats to  **Fire** , generating sudden heat-lift to enable the "Leap" onto enemy decks.

##### 4. The Five Guerrilla Clans: Specialized Kin

###### 1. The Iron-Briars ( Manis/Dasypodidae )

**Tactical Role** : Static Defense and Area Denial.

**Physiology** : Heavily armored with "Stone-Hide" (derived from  *Bison Cinereus*  wallowing rituals).

**Synergy** : Masters of "Stone-Root" and "Green-Slate" construction. They erect unassailable fortifications in minutes, securing extraction zones against terrestrial counter-attacks.

**Signature Gear** :  **Petrified Wood**  shields reinforced with  **Green-Slate**  plating, resistant to moisture and rot.

###### 2. The Crescent-Wings ( Lepidoptera )

**Tactical Role** : Aerial Reconnaissance and Night-Strike.

**Physiology** : Nocturnal kin utilizing "Starlight Synthesis" (derived from  *Moon-Flower*  biology).

**Synergy** : Their "Vibration-Sense" is supercharged by the "Chaos Surge" of the moon  **Cruorbus** . They are most lethal during  **Shadow Week** , detecting enemy heartbeats through ship hulls in total darkness.

**Signature Gear** :  **Chroma-Skin**  suits that mimic the shifting violet light of  **Shatterface** , providing passive optical camouflage.

###### 3. The Crimson Carapace ( Coccinellidae )

**Tactical Role** : Durable Shock Troopers.

**Physiology** : Stocky build with integrated "Chitin-Hull" plating.

**Synergy** : Specialized in "Iron Embrace" boarding tactics, physically grappling and crushing enemies while their  **Stone-Hide**  armor dissipates kinetic impact.

**Signature Gear** :  **Titanium** -infused carapace plating designed to withstand thermal "Blowouts."

###### 4. The Honorable Shadows ( Mephitidae )

**Tactical Role** : Chemical Warfare and "Blight" Suppression.

**Physiology** : Natural resistance to neurotoxins and biological contaminants.

**Synergy** : Though distinct from the  **Order of Purifiers** , the clan pays a "Silent Tithe" to the secret mountain monastery to train in the delivery of "Viper-Haze" via Pop-Bolts, disabling crews without damaging high-value cargo.

**Signature Gear** :  **Razor-cloth**  robes (woven from monastery reeds) that injure anyone attempting a grappling maneuver.

###### 5. The Knot-Cloak Dragoons ( Leporidae )

**Tactical Role** : High-Velocity Skirmishers.

**Physiology** : Extreme  *Motus*  (Reflex) and "Sky-Leaping" capabilities.

**Synergy** : Drive-by specialists who utilize high speed to strike and vanish. They master vertical mobility in the vertical crags of the  **Grind Canyons** .

**Signature Gear** :  **Silk-Steel Cables**  (farmed from  **Weaver-Cows** ) for high-tension grappling and  **Obsidian Daggers**  for high-speed slashing.

##### 5. Environmental Mastery: Navigating Chaos

**The Jitter-Rig Protocol**  Raider Navigators utilize a  **Jitter-Rig** —a raw  **Dragonstone**  shard surrounded by iron and quartz tuning forks—to feel the "texture of reality." By monitoring resonance shifts, Navigators can predict a "Meander" before it occurs. This allows Raiders to strike while an enemy vessel is struggling with a sudden 10-degree rotational shift, maintaining their own vector while the target's navigation fails.**Shadow Week Operations**  The 7-day eclipse of  **Shadow Week**  is the Raiders' primary operational window. While terrestrial powers like the  **Iron Caldera**  are in total lockdown, Raiders utilize the "Chaos-Free Corridors" generated by  **Ghost-Light Towers**  to strike high-value targets while the global "Tension Line" is at peak failure.**The Anti-Surveillance Edge**  By leveraging the "Slow-Energy Output" (derived from  *Bradypod*  metabolism), Raider teams remain invisible to "Life-Sense" magic and "Thermal Vision." Their ships are rigged with  **Iron-Weave Canvas**  and  **Silk-Steel Cables**  to run on low-energy stasis, allowing them to drift through  **Chaos Zones**  as non-biological signatures, bypassing the "Panopticon of the Deep" and other surveillance nets.

##### 6. Strict Prohibitions and Formatting

**Operational Sanitization** : Under no circumstances are  *Vulpines*  (Foxes),  *Simians*  (Monkeys), or  *Castoridae*  (Beavers) to be associated with Raider ports. Any sighting of these  **Canopy Clan**  or  **Twelve Crowns**  species near Raider assets is to be treated as a hostile incursion and met with immediate kinetic response.

**Conductor Alignment** : Strict adherence to the  **Twelve Elemental Conductors**  is mandatory. Incorrect alignment—such as utilizing  **Osmium**  to stop  **Fire**  (it must be  **Tungsten** )—will result in an immediate catastrophic  **Blowout**  and is classified as operational negligence.**DOCUMENT ENDS**
