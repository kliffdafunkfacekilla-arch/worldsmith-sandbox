# The Prism-Scale Collective (The Geode City)

**Aliases:** The Crystal Court, The Light-Bearers, The Refractors **Location:** The Quartz Coast (The Eastern jagged shoreline of translucent cliffs) **Patron Threat:** **[[The Mirror Lord]]** ([[Magistar Opecten]]) – Dragon of Paranoia, Surveillance, and Blinding Light. **Core Philosophy:** "Light reveals truth. To hide in the dark is to admit guilt."

## 1.0 Geography: The City of Refraction

Their capital, **Lumen-Spire**, is not built _on_ the rock; it is carved _into_ a colossal, semi-translucent Quartz formation.

- **The Architecture:**
    
    - **The Translucent Walls:** During the day, the sun hits the outer cliffs, and the light travels _through_ the quartz. The interior of the city glows with a soft, diffused white light.
        
    - **The Prism Effect:** Natural impurities in the rock split the light. Walking the streets is like walking inside a diamond. Rainbows are not weather phenomena here; they are architectural features.
        
- **The Defense:** **The Glare.**
    
    - They polish specific cliff faces to act as massive, crude reflectors. An approaching ship isn't hit by a weapon immediately; it is forced to sail into a blinding wall of white light that hides the city’s true layout and burns the retinas of the crew.
        

## 2.0 Biology & Caste: The Chromatic Hierarchy

They are Reptilian (Chameleons/Geckos/Skinks). Their biology dictates their social standing.

### **1. The Chromatics (The Nobility)**

- **Species:** Chameleons with active, high-speed chromatophores.
    
- **Role:** Leadership, Diplomacy, and Art.
    
- **Status:** Measured by **Vibrancy**. A leader must be able to hold a complex, shifting pattern on their skin for hours.
    
- **Communication:** "The Flash." They speak with words, but the _subtext_ is on their skin. (e.g., Saying "I agree" while flashing _Orange_ means "I am skeptical.")
    

### **2. The Monochromes (The Laborers)**

- **Species:** Bearded Dragons / Horned Toads (Dull, fixed scales).
    
- **Role:** They mine the quartz, polish the lenses, and haul the coal.
    
- **Treatment:** They are not slaves, but they are "Invisible." In a society obsessed with color, being brown or grey makes you essentially part of the furniture.
    

### **3. The Bleached (The Criminals)**

- **Punishment:** Criminals undergo chemical stripping of their scales. They turn bone-white and sensitive to light.
    
- **The Stigma:** They are walking ghosts. They are often exiled to the **[[Coastal Theocracy]]** or sold to the **[[Iron Caldera]]** as labor.
    

## 3.0 Government: The Grand Luminary

- **Title:** **The Grand Luminary**.
    
- **Selection:** Chosen by **Focus**.
    
    - The candidates stand in a circle. A massive lens focuses a beam of sunlight into the center. They must use mirrors and their own [[Lux]] magic to direct the light without burning themselves. The one who creates the most complex, stable pattern wins.
        
- **Current Luminary:** **Solas the Bright**. A paranoid Skink who covers himself in jewelry so he is always sparkling, terrified that Opecten will eat him if he goes dim.
    

## 4.0 Technology: The Archimedean War

They do not rely on sorcerous myths; they possess Concentrated Heat.

### **The Solar-Lance (Siege Weapon)**

- **Design:** A massive array of concave, polished quartz lenses mounted on a wagon or tower.
    
- **Function:** It requires a crew of six to align. It focuses sunlight into a single point about the size of a dinner plate.
    
- **Effect:** It acts as a "Focused Photonic Spear." It takes time to lock on (3-5 rounds). Once focused, it **melts** through wood, sailcloth, and even thin iron. It creates terrifying, silent burns.
    
- **Weakness:** Useless at night or in heavy fog (The **[[River Folk]]** fog-magic is their hard counter).
    

### **Handheld Optics**

- **The Flash-Shield:** A shield polished to a mirror finish. Used to blind opponents in melee or signal across battlefields.
    
- **The Spy-Glass:** They make the world's best telescopes.
    
- **The Fire-Starter:** Every soldier carries a "Focus-Pebble" (a perfectly cut lens) to start campfires instantly using the sun.
    

## 5.0 Economy: The Gem Trade

They export the raw materials of Opulence and Science.

- **Export 1: Industrial Quartz.**
    
    - **Piezoelectric Crystals:** Crystals that spark when struck. Sold to the **[[Iron Caldera]]** for igniting their engines.
        
    - **Hard-Glass:** Translucent stone used for reinforced windows in airships.
        
- **Export 2: Pigment & Dye bases.**
    
    - They mine rare mineral salts used to create vibrant paints.
        
- **Import:** **Coal/Fuel.** Their city is cold at night (stone loses heat fast). They import massive amounts of coal from the **[[Iron Caldera]]** to keep the internal braziers lit.
    

## 6.0 Key NPC: Artificer Lens (The Glass-Grinder)

- **Role:** Head of the Lens-Grinders Guild.
    
- **Species:** A heavily scarred Gecko (burn marks from working with hot glass).
    
- **Quirk:** He wears a leather apron with fifty pockets, each holding a different grade of sanding grit.
    
- **Agenda:** He wants to create a lens powerful enough to see _inside_ a Chaos Zone without going mad. He pays **[[Red Sash]]** adventurers top dollar to test his prototypes.
