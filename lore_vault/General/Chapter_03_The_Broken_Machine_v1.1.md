##### 3.1 Anatomy of a Reality Spike

The Sacred Groves are "Reality Spikes" deployed by the Engineer to anchor drifting continents. Each prison utilizes a four-layer containment strategy:

**Boundary (Storm Wall):**  A point of high-friction kinetic resistance where containment magic meet draconic will.

**Core (Impossible Serenity):**  Ancient Fae cities preserved in absolute stasis by the anti-chaos energy of sacrificed Elders.

**Cell (Dragonstone Building):**  Massive crystal formations containing the physical form of the Chaos Dragon.

##### 3.2 Geographic Catalog

**The Stillness (Stagus):**  Located in the Frozen North; an absolute zero environment where the  **Ice-Bound**  ensure total stagnation.

**The Heavy Lands (Tiraton):**  Situated on the Crushed Coast; defined by wild gravity that either rejects the earth or crushes matter into paste.

**The Haunting (Termhill):**  Found in the Gilded Steppe; a zone where travelers encounter their own temporal echoes or dead ancestors.

**The Melt (Aurgenas):**  Located at the Shifting Delta; a region of high liquidity where the landscape rearranges its material state daily.

##### 3.3 The Ritual of Recharging

The  **Mandate of the Chain**  is a militant, anti-magic organization dedicated to the eradication of supernatural power. Comprising the 50% of the population born without the magical "Spark," they possess zero internal Aetheric pressure, granting them an absolute, inherent biological immunity to the telepathic influence of the Fae, the Worm Cults, and the Chaos Dragons. In their misguided zeal, the Mandate attempts to "purge" the world of magic by absorbing ambient chaos into their heavy grounding chains of the twelve elemental metals. When these chains reach saturation, the Nulls haul them to the Sacred Groves to dump the power, genuinely believing they are bleeding the magic out of the world. By dumping this energy into the Chaos Zones, they feed the planetary digestive system—where chaos leaks from Cruorbus, is siphoned by the Dragonstone prisons, flows to the Convergence, and drains into the Void—inadvertently acting as the maintenance crew for the prison seals.

## Aetheric Tension

*Source file: `Magic and Religion/Aetheric Tension.md`*

> [!NOTE]
> **Dg-publish:** True

The Shattered World is a "Tensegrity Structure." The continents are not flying; they are held in a web of magnetic tension created by the flow of Chaos. the entire planet is cracked and would break apart without the tension of the chaos flows the tectonic plates floating on the super heated mantle act the same as any others with the added instability of the entire planet is itself in several pieces held against each other by outside tension like a broken sphere wrapped in rubber bands to hold it together.
## I. The Hydraulic Model (The Flow)

Chaos is not just background chaos-bleed; it is a physical, pressurized current.

### 1. The Circuit

- **The Source (Pressure):** **Shatterface (The Moon)**. It constantly bombards the planet with raw Aetheric matter.
    
- **The Stream (Current):** This matter doesn't dissipate; it flows in invisible, high-velocity rivers through the atmosphere and bedrock toward the path of least resistance.
    
- **The Sink (Suction):** **The 12 Dragon Prisons**. The imprisoned Dragons act as massive vacuum pumps, instinctively trying to consume the chaos to regain their power.
    
- **The Drain (Disposal):** **The [[Convergence]]**. The excess energy is pulled from the Prisons to the Center, where it spirals down the "World-Drain" ([[The Fae]] Portal) back into the Void.
    

### 2. The Physical Force: "The Drag"

- **The Mechanic:** This high-velocity flow creates **"Aetheric Drag."**
    
- **The Effect:** Just as a fast river pulls a boat downstream, the "River of Chaos" pulls the physical landmass it flows through toward the [[Convergence]].
    
- **The Result:** This constant inward pull counteracts the centrifugal force of the planet’s rotation, keeping the shattered planet breaking and  flying off into space.
    

## II. The Mechanics of Anchoring

The 12 Dragonstone Prisons are not just jails; they are the **Nails** holding the world together.

### 1. The Composition: "The World-Nails"

- **Material:** Solid, condensed **Dragonstone** (The Clockwork Debris). It is the only material dense enough to channel the Flow without eroding.
    
- **Structure:** They penetrate deep into the crust of their respective continents (e.g., [[Iron Caldera]] is built _on top_ of the Prison of Tiraton).
    

### 2. The Saturation Point ("The Bleed")

- **The Sponge Effect:** The Prison absorbs the Flow.
    
- **Saturation:** When the Moon is full (High Flow), the Prison cannot process the energy fast enough.
    
- **The Bleed:** Excess chaos spills out into the surrounding geography.
    
    - _Narrative Consequence:_ This creates the **Permanent [[Chaos Zones]]** around the Anchors. The land near a Prison is always mutated because the "plumbing" is leaking.
        

### 3. The Cult Threat: "The Breakers"

- **Worm Cult Doctrine:** They believe breaking the Prison frees the Dragon.
    
- **The Reality:** Breaking the Prison severs the Anchor. If they succeed, the continent loses its "Tension Line" to the [[Convergence]] and simply drifts off into the freezing void of space. They aren't liberators; they are unintentional nihilists.
    

## III. Tensegrity & Rotational Shift

### 2. The "Meander" (The Shift)

- **The Trigger:** A "Stream" changes course.
    
    - _Cause:_ A blockage (Magical buildup), a Lunar wobble, or a massive [[Sump-Kin]] explosion diverts the flow.
        
- **The Mechanics:** The shifting is not massive tectonic plates floating away or spinning 1280 degrees. Instead, it triggers localized earthquakes, minor elevation changes at the faults, and a slight shift of only 1 or 2 degrees.
    
    - _Effect on Infrastructure:_ This minor orientation shift is enough to cause a straight road to veer to the side slightly.
    
    - _The Real Danger (Mountain Routes):_ Mountains, which are the edges of plates pushing against each other, change their profiles drastically enough during these shifts that traveling over them by foot or airship is a blind guess at the routes.
    
    - _The Real Danger (Chaos Flows):_ The tectonic shift causes chaos flows to reroute themselves, shifting sometimes a solid mile or two to the left or right, which can block other non-mountain routes.


## IV. Industrial Interaction: Tapping the Flow

Factions attempt to exploit this physics engine, with varying degrees of lethality.

### 1. The Iron Caldera: "Geothermal" vs. "Geo-Aetheric"

- They don't just use lava heat. They likely tap the **"Friction Heat"** generated by the flow of Chaos grinding against the Dragonstone Anchor of Tiraton.
    
    

### 3. The Sky-Barons: "Slip-Streaming"

- Smugglers use **"Drafting"**. They fly their skiffs _inside_ the upper edge of a Flow Stream.
    
- **Risk:** High chaos-bleed.
    
- **Reward:** The "Drag" propels the ship at double speed without using fuel. They are surfing the river that holds the world together.
