# Magistar Lophex

> **"The Hoarder of Void"**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School** | [[Anumis]] (Knowledge) |
| **Element** | Bismuth |
| **Original Title** | The Keeper |
| **Dragon Form** | [[The Geometric Void]] |
| **Pre-Corruption Name** | Eldra |

## The Rise
Lophex cataloged and locked away every secret stolen from the other species. She believed that possessing a secret gave her ownership over the reality it described. She was the living library of the Dominion, her mind stained with the stolen lore of a thousand broken cultures.

## The Tyranny
She enforced ignorance. To know something forbidden was a capital offense. She used her hoard of knowledge to build the world-breaking weapon, cross-referencing Fae lore to find the weakness in the [[Celestial Clockwork]]. She turned knowledge into a weapon of domination, "The Mental Scourge".

## The Corruption
When she tried to learn the mind of the cosmos, it broke her. She became **The Geometric Void**, a dragon of Bismuth crystals and impossible angles. She does not breathe fire; she breathes "Arcane Nullification," unravelling the logic and science of her enemies.
