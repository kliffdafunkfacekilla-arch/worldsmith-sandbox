**AURA (Charm)**

- **The Power:** Aura is the Resonance, the electromagnetic frequency of the soul. It is the music of the spheres that dictates emotion. A wielder of Aura adjusts their own spiritual frequency to harmonize or dissonate with the beings around them.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Spirit):** You project a dissonant frequency that shears the soul from the body, causing psychic anguish, or you induce primal Fear or Rage to force enemies to flee or attack their allies.
        
    - **Defense:** You project a field of absolute harmony (Sanctuary) that makes it mentally impossible for entities to attack you, or you bolster the resolve of your allies against fear.
        
    - **Utility:** You can Charm others by matching their wavelength, communicate telepathically via emotional resonance, or implant suggestions into the subconscious mind.
