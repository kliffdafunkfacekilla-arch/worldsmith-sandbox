**The Failure:** The Avians attempted to replicate the Quill-Kin's medicine but lacked the "Death Cult" alchemical knowledge. The result was **"Thunder-Swill"** (Crackly Moonshine)—a hyper-potent, fizzy alcohol that causes temporary paralysis ("tips a bear over").

• **The Innovation:** Instead of scrapping the failure, the Avian merchant class realized the "fizzy nature" (static charge) of the liquid acted as a **hyper-distributor**.

    ◦ **The Science:** The static charge forces pigment deep into the molecular structure of the fabric.

• **The Product: "Avian Silk" (The Prism-Weave)**

    ◦ **Qualities:** Fabrics dyed this way are unnaturally vibrant. They don't just reflect light; they seem to _hum_ with color. They never fade.

    ◦ **The Status Symbol:** Owning a dress or coat made of Avian Silk is the ultimate sign of wealth.

    ◦ **The Market:**

        ▪ **The Hive Queens:** The **[[Hive Commonwealth]]** is obsessed with fashion-driven markets. The Queens likely bid wars over exclusive bolts of "Avian Azure" or "High-Altitude Crimson."

        ▪ **The Highborn:** The **[[Equine Nobles]]** of the Heartland would demand these silks for their Senate robes.

**3. The "Thunder-Swill" Black Market**

While the Avians officially use the "Crackly Moonshine" for industrial dye, a substance that can "tip a bear over" inevitably finds a drinker.
