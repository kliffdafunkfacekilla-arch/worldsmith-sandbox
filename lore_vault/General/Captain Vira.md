# Captain Vira ("The Dive-Bomber")

> **"If the wings are still attached, it's still flying. Now, drop the ballast and hang on!"**

## 1.0 Command Profile
Captain of **[[The Felicitous Falcon]]**, the oldest and smallest of the Sovereign Barges. Vira is the "Ace" of the fleet, representing the daring spirit of the **[[Cloud-Harriers]]** rather than the stuffy politics of the Roosts.

* **Species:** Peregrine Falcon.
* **Visuals:** Small, aerodynamic, and twitchy. She wears a tight leather flight suit rather than admiral's robes. She has wind-burn scars around her eyes from flying without goggles at high altitude.

## 2.0 The "Sky-Sister"
Vira is the only Imperial figure respected by the **[[Free Sky-Baronies]]**.
* **The Falcon's Quirk:** Her barge has only one Cutter Bay.
* **The Tactic:** Unlike other Admirals who command from the bridge, Vira pilots the single deployed Cutter herself. She is famous for the **"Needle-Thread" maneuver**—flying her fighter *through* the superstructure of an enemy airship to drop a Black-Powder charge internally.
