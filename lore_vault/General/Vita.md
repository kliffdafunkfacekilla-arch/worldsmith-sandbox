**VITA (Vitality)**

- **The Power:** Vita is the chaotic biological spark, the energy of cellular division and evolution. It is the wet, teeming directive of life to survive at any cost. A wielder of Vita connects to the bio-electric network of all living things, acting as a master gardener of flesh.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Poison):** You weaponize biology, commanding an enemy’s cells to attack themselves (auto-immune failure), causing rapid tumor growth, or flooding their veins with synthesized toxins.
        
    - **Defense:** You supercharge your own regeneration, knitting wounds shut in seconds, or mutate your skin into chitin or bark to absorb blows.
        
    - **Utility:** You can heal the injured, accelerate the growth of plants to create bridges, or link the life-force of the party together to share the burden of survival.
