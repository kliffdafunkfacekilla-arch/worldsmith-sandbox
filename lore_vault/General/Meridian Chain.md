# 📂 File: The Meridian Chain (The Ghost-Light Cartel)

**Aliases:** The Wick-Keepers, The Snail-Lords, The Unseen Engine

**Location:** The Eastern & Southern Coastlines (The Blue-Route)

**Patron Threat:** **[[Magistar Carulkem]] ([[Magistar Carulkem]])** – Dragon of **[[Nexus1]] (Fortitude/Matter)**.

**Core Philosophy:** "Mutual Profit is the only Universal Language."

## 1.0 The Engine of Commerce: The Supercargo

The Chain operates on a system of absolute accountability. The lynchpin of this system is the **Supercargo**.

- **The Role:** A specialized Penguin agent assigned to a single cargo pod for its _entire_ journey. They are not the captain of the ship; they are the **Guardian of the Goods**.
    
- **The Trinity of Duty:** A Supercargo must be three things simultaneously:
    
    1. **The Lawyer:** They enforce contracts and arbitrate disputes on the high seas.
        
    2. **The Diplomat:** They negotiate passage through contested waters and secure trade terms at every port.
        
    3. **The Guardian:** They are combat-trained to protect the cargo from pirates, monsters, or corruption.
        
- **The Secret Mandate:** While their public duty is the cargo, their _true_ standing order (as members of the Order of the Wick) is to acquire **Intel on Dragonstone Prisons** at any cost.
    

## 2.0 The Infrastructure: Bridge-Cities

The Chain does not just visit ports; they _build_ the chokepoints.

- **The Strategy:** They construct massive, fortified settlements at the exact points where major rivers meet the ocean or where trade routes bottleneck.
    
- **The Function:** These are the only "Safe Ports" in the region, anchored by a **Ghost-Light Tower** that keeps the local reality stable.
    
- **The Layout:**
    
    - **The Center (The Lock-Gates):** The heart of the city is dominated by the Chain’s own operations—massive docks, Snail-pens, and cargo locks.
        
    - **The Flanks (The Service Zone):** Surrounding the docks are inns, supply depots, and markets designed to service land-based caravans.
        
- **The Control:** If you want to move goods from land to sea, you _must_ pass through a Bridge-City. This gives the Chain a stranglehold on the regional economy.
    

## 3.0 The Propulsion: Snail-Draught

- **The Engine:** Colossal **Abyssal Snails** (bio-engineered beasts the size of houses).
    
- **The Mechanic:** They swim deep beneath the surface or crawl along the sea floor, tethered to the surface barges by immense iron chains.
    
- **The Advantage:**
    
    - **Weather Immunity:** Snails ignore surface winds and waves.
        
    - **Torque:** They provide slow, unstoppable pulling power. A Snail-Draught convoy can drag heavy iron-barges through a hurricane without slowing down.
        
- **The Wranglers:** Expert **Walruses** and **Seals** manage the beasts, communicating with them via clicks and thumps.
    

## 4.0 The Ghost-Light Network (The Terraforming Project)

- **The Tech:** Derived from stolen knowledge of the **Sacred Groves**.
    
- **The Effect:** Each Lighthouse projects an **Anti-Chaos Field**.
    
    - **Safe-Zones:** Half the radius covers the water, creating a "clean" shipping lane; the other half covers the Bridge-City, protecting it from time-warps.
        
- **The Goal:** They are slowly building towers up and down the coast. The ultimate plan is to link the fields, creating a continuous **Chaos-Free Corridor**—effectively terraforming the ocean into a zone of pure Law.
    
- **The Order of the Wick:** The Penguin caste who maintain these towers. They use complex **Flash-Codes** to signal warnings of incoming Chaos surges to the fleets.
    

## 5.0 Leadership: The Hexarchy

- **Public Face:** **Grand-Link Nero** (Emperor Penguin). A charismatic diplomat who manages the "Mutual Profit" politics.
    
- **The Secret Council:** Nero and five others (Elders of the Walrus, Seal, and Otter kin) form the true leadership.
    
- **The Shadow Assets:**
    
    - **The [[Ghostwind Raiders]]:** The Council secretly owns and funds this "Pirate" fleet to conduct Black Ops and asset denial, while publicly denouncing them.
        
    - **The Warden Connection:** The Chain secretly supplies the **[[Grey Wardens]]** (via "lost" cargo drops) in exchange for deep data on the Dragonstone Prisons.
        

## 6.0 The "Links" (Operational Teams)

The fleet is divided into specialized teams based on environment.

- **Granite Link (North/Ice):** Walrus-Kin hauling ore.
    
- **Coral Link (South/Reefs):** Otters and Rockhoppers moving spice.
    
- **Abyssal Link (Deep Ocean):** Elephant Seals managing the trans-oceanic Snail-Draughts.
    
- **Delta Link (Rivers):** Dolphins and Gentoos navigating the toxic Sump.
    
- **Ghost Link (Black Ops):** Macaroni Penguins and Orcas running the [[Ghostwind Raiders]].
