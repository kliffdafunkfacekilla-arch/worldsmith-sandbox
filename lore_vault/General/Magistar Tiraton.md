## The Rise
Tiraton was a warlord who believed that "Truth" was simply whatever force could not be moved. He commanded the legions that oversaw the deep mines and resource extraction, physically demolishing ancient Fae settlements to use their rubble for the Magistars' palaces.

## The Tyranny
His rule was defined by crushing physical oppression. He forced the species skilled in metalworking to labor in brutal, subterranean mines to arm his legions. He viewed all political resistance as a simple engineering problem—one that required the application of sufficient force to level.

## The Corruption
Upon [[The Shattering]], the concept of "Might" inverted upon him. He became **The World-Weight**, a legless wyrm of ultra-dense Osmium. He is now so heavy that he crushes reality by existing, swimming through the air by rejecting the gravity of the earth beneath him.
