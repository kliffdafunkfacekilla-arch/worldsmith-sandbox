## The Defection
The chief inquisitor for [[Magistar Carulkem]]. His job was to break the will of any who doubted the Magistars. While torturing a Fae prisoner, he was shown a vision of the world's beauty before the hubris of man. He walked out of the dungeon, released every prisoner, and became the spiritual anchor of the rebellion.
