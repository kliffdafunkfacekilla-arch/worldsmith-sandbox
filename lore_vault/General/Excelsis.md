# Excelsis Citadel (The Avian Capital)

**Region:** The Engineer's Mountain Range (The Highest Peaks) **Role:** Seat of the Emperor / Cultural Heart of the Empire **Vibe:** Vertigo, Sunlight, and Judgment

## 1.0 The Vertical City

Capital of the [[Avian Empire]] It is carved directly into the sheer cliffs of the three highest peaks (The Triumvirate).

- **The "Streets":** There are no streets. There are only "Drafts"—updrafts of wind explicitly mapped for travel between spires.
    
- **The Landings:** Narrow ledges of polished marble jutting over a 10,000-foot drop.
    
    - **The Guest Problem:** Non-flying dignitaries must be winched up in gilded cages or ride the terrifying **"Ascension Lifts"** (counter-weighted cable cars run by strong flightless birds).
        

## 2.0 Key Districts

- **The High Roost (The Senate):** An open-air amphitheater at the very summit. Senators debate while exposed to the freezing wind and blinding sun. To shiver is a sign of weakness; to squint is a sign of dishonesty.
    
- **The Mirror-Gardens:** Because trees don't grow this high, the Avians build "trees" out of glass and mirrors to refract the light. It is a blindingly beautiful, sterile garden.
    
- **The Undercroft (The Flightless Quarter):** Deep inside the mountain are the warm, torch-lit tunnels where the flightless castes (Ducks, Chickens, Cassowaries) live. This is the industrial engine of the city—warm, loud, and smelling of baking bread and forge-smoke.
