**The Apex Predator: "The Obsidian Razor-Strider"**

• **Classification:** _Struthio obsidianus_. terrifying pack-hunting bird in [[Iron Caldera]] desert

• **Physiology:**

    ◦ The Build: Standing 7-8 feet tall, these flightless birds possess massive, scaled legs capable of outrunning a steam-engine train over broken terrain.

    ◦The Plumage: They do not have soft feathers. They are covered in **quills of biological volcanic glass**. These feathers are jet black and razor-sharp.

        ▪ _Auditory Warning:_ When a pack runs, their feathers clack together, creating a sound like wind chimes in a gale. If you hear the chimes, the pack is already flanking you.

    ◦ The Beak: A "Shearing Beak" evolved specifically to crack the heavy armor of Gritbores . It functions like a hydraulic rescue tool (Jaws of Life), delivering immense pressure to a singular point to snap bone and plate.

• **Tactics:**

    ◦ The "Glass-Storm": They hunt in packs of 6-12. They do not bite immediately; they body-check. They run alongside prey and slam their flanks into them. Their glass feathers act like a cheese grater, shredding the target's side (or tires/canvas covers of vehicles) in a drive-by slash.

    ◦ The Cull: Once the prey is bleeding or immobilized, the Alpha delivers the shearing bite to the neck.

• **Economic/Cultural Use:**

    ◦ "Feather-Knives" Molted feathers are incredibly sharp but brittle. They are prized by Ironborn assassins or Quill-Kin surgeons as single-use scalpels that are sharper than steel.
