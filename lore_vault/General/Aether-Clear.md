**The Refined Medicine: "Aether-Clear" (The Anti-Chaos Draught)**

The revelation that fermented and distilled [[Ozone-Milk]] has **Anti-Chaos effects** changes the geopolitical value of the **[[Avian Empire]]** considerably.

• **The Process:** You specified it requires **Fermentation + Distillation + A Second Ingredient**.

    ◦ _The Logic:_ The fermentation breaks down the volatile static proteins. The distillation concentrates the essence. The "Second Ingredient" acts as the stabilizer.

• **The Use Case:** It treats **"Corruption Sickness"** (the physical mutations caused by exposure to [[Chaos Zones]]). It doesn't cure a full transformation, but it halts the spread and clears the "mental fog" caused by dragons like **Valkor** (Mental Scourge) or **Ghal** (Haunting

**The Secret:** The precise fermentation ratios of **[[Ozone-Milk]]** and **Ghost Flowers** remain the exclusive knowledge of the **Quill-Kin**.

• **The Cover:** Because the Quill-Kin are already the world's apothecaries and alchemists, buying bulk herbs is standard operating procedure. No one notices the Ghost Flowers hidden among the crates of ginseng and bandages.

• **The Patient's Apathy:** As you noted, the desperate don't ask questions. The "gross syrup" aspect serves as a security feature—if it tasted good, people would try to replicate it for recreation. By keeping it medicinal and foul, the Quill-Kin ensure only the sick seek it out.

• **The Leverage:** This monopoly makes the Quill-Kin untouchable. Even the **[[Avian Empire]]** cannot strong-arm them, because if the Quill-Kin stop brewing, the "Corruption Sickness" (mutations from [[Chaos Zones]]) becomes a pandemic.
