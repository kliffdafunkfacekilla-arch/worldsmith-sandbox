**MASS (Might)**

- **The Power:** Mass is the anchor of reality, governing the generation of gravitons and the density of matter. A wielder of Mass treats gravity not as a constant, but as a variable slider. They feel heavier than the world around them, their footsteps cracking the pavement as they manipulate the sheer weight of existence.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Force):** You generate crushing kinetic impacts, turning a simple punch into a siege weapon, or creating localized gravity wells that implode enemies into dense spheres of gore.
        
    - **Defense:** You increase your own density to become an immovable object, allowing kinetic energy to ripple harmlessly through your molecular lattice.
        
    - **Utility:** You can negate gravity to float, lift impossible tonnage by reducing an object's weight to zero, or walk up walls by shifting your personal gravitational down-point.
