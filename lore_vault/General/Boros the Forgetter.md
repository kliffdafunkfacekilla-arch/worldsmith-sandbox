## The Defection
[[Magistar Tyrustis]]’s enforcer of work-quotas. He was known for being heartless, until he was ordered to kill his own father for being "inefficient". Boros used his immense willpower to shield his father's spirit, eventually leading a mass exodus of laborers into the relative safety of the underground tunnels.
