# **Shadow-Seal Ink**

**Shadow-Seal Ink** is a specialized, magically binding ink used by Avian Notaries within the Avian Empire and the Gilded Compass Cartel to execute immutable business agreements.

### **Composition & Production**
- Sourced and processed exclusively by **Avian Notaries**.
- The raw ink is blended with a precise ratio of refined **Dragonstone Dust (D-Dust)**.
- It requires an **Ink-Wright** to perform the binding tattoo ritual during the signing of a high-stakes **Shadow-Set Contract**.

### **The Silver Scar**
- As the terms of the contract are fulfilled, the ink physically reacts with the signee's body, slowly fading.
- Once the contract is fully satisfied, the ink completely disappears, leaving behind a faint, permanent **Silver Scar**.
- In the commerce culture of Ostraka, these scars serve as a physical resume of reliability. A "clean" merchant who has no scars has no history of completing verified high-stakes deals, and is therefore completely untrusted.

### **Legal & Safety Status**
- **Legal Status:** Fully legal and highly regulated under Avian mercantile law.
- **Safety Hazard:** Carries a minor surgical risk during application due to the volatile properties of the D-Dust in the ink, which can cause localized static burns or nerve twitching if not properly sigil-shielded.
