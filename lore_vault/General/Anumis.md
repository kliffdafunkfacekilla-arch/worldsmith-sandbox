**ANUMIS (Knowledge)**

- **The Power:** Anumis is the Source Code of reality, the raw data that defines the Clockwork. It is the "Magic" that exists outside of physics. A wielder of Anumis acts as a programmer, reading the file headers of the universe and executing scripts that bypass natural laws.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Arcane):** You fire beams of raw, violet data that rewrite the target's existence, bypassing physical armor to delete their Hit Points directly, or binding them with runic chains.
        
    - **Defense:** You write sub-routines that counter enemy spells, create wards that forbid entry to supernatural creatures, or shield yourself in geometric force fields.
        
    - **Utility:** You can summon entities from the database of other planes, teleport across vast distances via the network, or read any language by accessing the universal lexicon.
