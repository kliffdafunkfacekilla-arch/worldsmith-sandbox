# Magistar Metrion

> **"The Architect of Zero"**

| Attribute | Detail |

| **School** | [[Ratio1]] (Logic) |
| **Element** | Silicon |

## The Rise
Metrion was the "Whisperer" who first convened the warlords on the Crag of Final Judgement. He did not view the war against [[The Fae]] as a battle of passion, but as an equation to be balanced. He posited that [[The Fae]] were "excess variables" and that the universe was a math problem he alone could solve.

## The Tyranny
As the leader of the Dominion, Metrion enforced absolute, cold logic. He viewed his subjects not as people, but as variables in a grand equation. He calculated the exact caloric and magical output of every citizen, enforcing a tax on "inefficiency." Resistance was deemed illogical and therefore punished with immediate deletion.

## The Corruption
When [[The Shattering]] occurred, Metrion’s mind attempted to process the infinite data of the void. His logic collapsed into the senseless, infinite math of chaos. He transformed into **The Crystalline Mind**, a dragon of silicon and circuitry who breathes lightning that rewrites the brains of his victims, turning them into mindless drones in his infinite calculation.
