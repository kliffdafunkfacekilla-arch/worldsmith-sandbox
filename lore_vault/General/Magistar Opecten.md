# Magistar Opecten

> **"The Paranoiac Eye"**

| Attribute | Detail |

| **School** | [[Lux]] (Awareness) |
| **Element** | Chromium |

## The Rise
Opecten’s hubris was the desire to see everything without being seen. He built a surveillance state that reached into the very dreams of his subjects. He oversaw the magical currents, ensuring no act of clandestine communication went undetected.

## The Tyranny
He abolished privacy, declaring it a sin against the Dominion. His gaze accelerated the decay of free will; to be watched by Opecten was to be paralyzed. He used his power to blackmail the other Magistars and terrorize the populace, ensuring total obedience.

## The Corruption
The light he used to watch the world blinded him. He became **The Mirror Lord**, a dragon of polished Chromium. He reflects the world twisted in his scales. His gaze now causes "The Clockwork Rot," accelerating time and space to dust.
