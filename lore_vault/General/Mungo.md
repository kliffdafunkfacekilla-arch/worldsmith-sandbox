# The Mungo (The Serpent-Breakers)

**Common Name:** Mongoose-Folk **Scientific Designation:** _Herpestes Titanus_ **Role:** Leather-Barons, Beast-Wranglers, Shock-Troopers **Home City:** **The Coil-Yards** (A grimy, industrial district of suspended cages and tannery vats).

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance

- **Physiology:** They are lean, wire-muscled, and stand about 4 to 5 feet tall. They are not built for heavy lifting, but for explosive speed.
    
- **Reflexes:** They possess the fastest twitch-muscle fibers in the Tangle. A Mungo can dodge a strike that hasn't even fully started yet. To a human eye, they move in a blur.
    
- **Immunity:** They have a high natural resistance to nerve-banes. A bite from a standard viper might make a Mungo dizzy for an hour, but it won't kill them. This makes them the only species capable of working the "Venom-Lines."
    
- **Visuals:** Their fur is short, coarse, and usually grey or brown (utilitarian). Most Mungos are covered in scars—badges of honor from the pits.
    

## 2.0 Psychology & Culture

- **The Philosophy of "The Snap":** Mungo culture is built on the concept of the "Snap"—the split-second moment where you either kill or die. They live life on a razor's edge. They despise hesitation.
    
- **Rivalry:** They have an intense cultural disdain for the **Serpentes** (The Snake-Folk Enforcers). While they don't hunt the sapient Snake-Folk for leather (that’s murder), they mock them relentlessly. A Mungo business strategy is often simply: "Whatever the Snake is selling, we sell it cheaper and faster."
    
- **Status:** Respect is earned by the size of the beast you have "broken." The Guildmasters wear cloaks made from the heads of King-Boas.
    

## 3.0 Industrial Role: The Leather-Barons

- **The [[Titan-Boa]] Pits:** They farm **Titan-Boas** (massive, cattle-eating snakes) in giant suspended cages.
    
- **The Harvest:** This is not a safe job. "Culling Day" involves a team of ten Mungos entering the cage with hooks and stunning-batons to wrestle a 2-ton predator into submission.
    
- **The Tannery:** They run the vats where **Viper-Leather** is cured. The smell of ammonia and tanning fluids clings to them, marking them as industrial workers.
    

## 4.0 Military Role: The Anti-Ambush Specialists

- **Style:** Counter-Attack.
    
- **Weaponry:**
    
    - **The "Fang-Hook":** A curved sickle-sword designed to hook an enemy's limb or weapon and redirect it.
        
    - **Twin Daggers:** Used for close-quarters finishing moves.
        
- **Tactics:** Mungos are hired to guard caravans against stealth attacks. Because their reflexes are so fast, they are the only ones who can react to a "Flux-Shift" or a sudden ambush from the **[[Eastern Hounds]]**. They strike before the enemy reveals themselves.
    

## 5.0 Social Quirks

- **The Stare:** Mungos do not blink during conversation. It is a dominance display. Looking away is a sign of submission.
    
- **Hyper-Metabolism:** Like the Monkeys, they burn energy fast. They eat constantly—usually strips of dried jerky or protein-bars made from Weevil-meat.
    
- **Personal Space:** They have none. They stand uncomfortably close to people. In their culture, distance implies you are preparing to dodge an attack. Standing close means "I trust you not to bite me."
