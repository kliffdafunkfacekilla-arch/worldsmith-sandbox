# The Celestial Codex & The Warden's Reckoning

**Subject:** Astronomy, Timekeeping, and Historical Eras **Status:** **[UPDATED 1650 A.W.]**

## 1.0 The Celestial Bodies

### **The World: Ostraka (formerly Elyia)**

A world cracked and broken by the attempt at wielding the force of creation ,its tectonic plates  held together by  the primordial chaos energy flowing from anchor points to the convergence where it is channeled into the celestial clockwork. without this tension the the physical planet itself would break apart and drift into the void of space.

### **The Primary Moon: Cruorbus (The Blood Orphan)**

- **Aliases:** _The Intruder, The Broken One, 
    
- **Appearance:** A tidally locked, shattered dwarf planet.
    
    - **Resting State:** Glows a vivid **Magenta-Purple** (The Bruise).
        
    - **Surge State:** Bleeds **Crimson Red** (The Hemorrhage) during high chaos or orbital stress.
        
- **Orbital Mechanic:** It does not rotate normally. It **wobbles** horizontally and precesses vertically, exposing its "Wound" (The Chaos Core) to the world in a rhythmic cycle that defines the seasons.
    

### **The Ghost Moon: Erranith (The Wandering Phantom)**

- **Orbit:** Highly elliptical. Returns only once every generation (approx. 30-33 years).
    
- **Effect:** Its gravity tears at Cruorbus, causing "The Tearing" (Meteor showers of Dragonstone).

## 3.0 The Seasonal Cycle (The Wobble)

Because Cruorbus wobbles twice a year (Upper Cycle and Lower Cycle), Ostraka experiences **Eight Seasons**.

### **Cycle A: The Upper Wobble**

_The Moon's damage is visible on the Northern/Upper Hemisphere._

- **1. Shadowburn (Summer 1):** 84 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ The "Open Eye" faces the world. Heat and Chaos are high.
        
- **2. Dryspell (Autumn 1):** 42 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ The Wound rotates away. The world dries out as magic recedes.
        
- **3. Frostin (Winter 1):** 49 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ The "Clean Side" faces the world. Cold, stable, grey skies.
        
- **4. GreenSpan (Spring 1):** 84 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ The Moon begins to wobble back, exposing the "Leering" edge. Rapid growth and mutation.
        

### **Cycle B: The Lower Wobble**

_The Moon has precessed vertically; the damage is now on the Southern/Lower Hemisphere._

- **5. Highreach (Summer 2):** 112 Days (Longest Season).
    
    - _The Phase:_ The "Weeping Eye." High tides, storms, and liquid chaos.
        
- **6. Spurium (Autumn 2):** 77 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ False Autumn. A mix of rotting vegetation and erratic magic.
        
- **7. Dimfreeze (Winter 2):** 84 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ The "Crown." The Moon is dark but crowned with a purple halo. Deep cold.
        
- **8. Shadowfall (The Eclipse):** 7 Days.
    
    - _The Phase:_ Total Eclipse. The "Reset" of the wobble.

## 4.0 Cultural Impact: The Shadow Week

_Occurs during the 7 days of Shadowfall. A global test of philosophy._

- **The Iron Caldera:** **Defiance.** They lock down and throw the "Festival of Life," consuming stockpiles in a riot of light and noise to mock the dark.
    
- **The Ursine Hegemony:** **Vigilance.** The "Hearthguard's Vigil." Young cubs prove their worth by keeping the hearth-fires lit for 7 days straight.
    
- **The River Folk:** **Community.** They float thousands of lanterns on the toxic rivers. A gap in the light chain signals danger upstream.
    
- **The Coastal Theocracy:** **Faith.** They chant at magma vents to keep the boiling tides back, believing the "Deep Ones" are testing them.
    
- **The Vaneer Concord:** **Logistics.** A mandatory audit and inventory period behind military shield-walls.
    
- **The Avian Empire:** **Profit.** The "Minting Season." Aetherium currency can only be forged in total magical darkness, so the banks run 24/7.

## 5.0 The Timeline of Ostraka

### **Era B.S. (Before Shattering)**

- **Ends in -6650 B.S.**
    
- **[[The Era of Order]]:** [[The Fae]] Ascendancy and the stewardship of [[The Sacred Groves]] (Aethelgard).
    
- **The Rise of Man:** The "Ancients" (Humans) transition from Fae students to technological rivals.
    
- **The Unified Dominion:** The 12 Magistars unite and enslave the world, stripping resources.
    

### **Era S.S. (The Shattered Sky)**

- **-6650 to -3000 B.S.**
    
- **The Great Shattering:** The Magistars attempt to seize godhood. The "Intruder Planet" (Cruorbus) is pulled from the void and collides with the moon.
    
- **The Scouring:** The Magistars warp into Chaos Dragons. Civilization collapses.
    

### **Era A.W. (After Warden)**

- **Year 1 A.W.:** **[[The Divine Sacrifice]].** The Engineer God collapses to form the world's geography. [[The First Wardens]] bind the Dragons to the Groves.
- **~850 A.W.:** **The Null Mandate.** Terrified by magical mutations, non-magical hunters unearth a damaged Warden archive. Misinterpreting the grounding vectors, they forge the anti-magic **Mandate of the Chain**.
- **~950 A.W.:** **The Hour of Vengeance.** The Scute, enslaved by Feathered Lizards, unearth ancient explosives. The Black Scale 7 collapse their masters' mountain, liberating their people and founding the **Scute Confederacy**.
- **Year 1000 A.W.:** The **Avian Empire** bypasses the deadly terrestrial Chaos Zones using flight. They establish an absolute global monopoly on logistics, founding the Fulcrum Bank to control the world's wealth.
- **Year 1500 A.W.:** Ground-dwelling Sparkborn refine the 12 elemental conductors, inventing the first crude **Aether-Skiffs**. The Avian monopoly on flight is violently shattered overnight.
- **~1520 A.W.:** **The Siege of Snowpeak.** Desperate for fireproof Stone-Root for their new engines, the Iron Caldera marches across the boiling Anvil-Run via a Tungsten Slag-Pontoon. The northern tribes unite to repel them, signing the Treaty of the Hearth and officially forging the **Ursine Hegemony**.
- **Year 1960 A.W.:** **The Exile of Koda.** General Koda of the Ursine Hegemony is branded with the Sigil of Ice for breaking protocol to save refugees. Exiled, she turns to sky-piracy.
- **Year 1985 A.W.:** **The Poker Game.** [[Mama Koda]] wins the deed to the tavern "Undine's Undying Dance" in a game of Dead Man's Poker.
- **Year 2000 A.W. (Current Year):** The [[Order of the Clockwork]] monitors the wobbling moon; The [[DEVIL'S ISLAND INSURGENCY]] prepares for a new contract.
