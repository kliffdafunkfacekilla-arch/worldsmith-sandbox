# Magistar Tyrustis

> **"The Slave King"**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School** | [[Lex]] (Willpower) |
| **Element** | Lithium |
| **Original Title** | The Unbroken |

| **Pre-Corruption Name** | Zeila |

## The Rise
Tyrustis was the engine of the Dominion's labor. She believed that enough Willpower could overwrite the laws of physics. She forced her own spirit to stay in a rotting host body for centuries through sheer stubbornness, refusing to die.

## The Tyranny
She used her power to enforce brutal work quotas and mentally break the spirit of resistance in the subjugated species. She viewed the world as something that must submit to her will; if reality disagreed with her, reality was wrong.

## The Corruption
Her mind shattered under the weight of the infinite void. She became **The Mad King**, a dragon of volatile Lithium. Her will is absolute but erratic; she acts as a "Void" that strips the identity out of everyone around her, imposing laws of physics that change moment to moment.
