# ---![[mousecleric.png]]

**☀️ Faction Profile: The Church of the Eternal Dawn**

*(The Solarium / The Light-Bringers / The Sanction)*

"Chaos is the shadow. We are the flame. A fire uncontrolled burns the house down; a fire in the hearth warms the family. Submit your Spark to the Church, and we will build a hearth for you. Keep it to yourself, and you will burn."  
— High Pontiff "Glimmer-Eye" (A Blind Albino Mouse)

## ---

**1.0 Overview: The State Religion**

The Church is the supreme spiritual authority of [[Heartland Alliance]]. It is the only organization allowed to train and sanction Magic Users.Mostly a force of good  and helpful to communities however there Grey Wicks , are seen as over zealous and generally disliked, the church powers constantly have to reign in on the orders zealot behavior.

* **The Dogma:** **Order through Light.**  
  * They believe the Sun is the ultimate expression of Order.  
  * They teach that Magic is dangerous and chaotic by nature. It must be "Yoked" (controlled) by scripture and discipline.  
* **The Role:** They run the **Schools**, the **Orphanages**, and the **Hospitals** (alongside Porcupines).  
* **The Politics:** They are the third power block. The Nobles have the Land, the [[Wolves]] have the Guns, but the Church has the **Hearts (and the Wizards)**.

## ---

**2.0 The "Tithe of Sparks" (Recruitment)**

* **The Law:** By law, any child born with "The Spark" (Magical Talent) must be handed over to the Church for training.  
  * **Noble Foals:** A Horse Noble born with magic is removed from the line of succession and becomes a High Bishop.  
  * **Peasant Fawns:** A Deer farmer's child with magic is taken to a Cathedral to become a Cleric.  
* **The Result:** The clergy is all Power users and one of the sources of its use in the heartland culture

## ---

**3.0 Structure: The Hierarchy of Light**

### **The High Clergy (The Robes)**

* **Species:** Mostly **Horses** (Nobles) and **Raccoons** (Charismatic Preachers).  
* **Role:** They manage the massive Cathedrals, conduct the Sunday Services, and advise the Senate.  
* **Aesthetic:** White and Gold robes, heavy sun-iconography, and lots of stained glass.

### **The Paladin Orders (The Swords of Dawn)**

* **Species:** Primarily **[[Mice]] and racoons, some [[Beavers]]**.  
  * *Why?* [[Mice]] are devout, fast, and zealous.  
* **The Visual:** A Mouse Paladin in full, shining plate armor, wielding a needle-thin rapier or a war-hammer made of a single bolt. They are adorable but terrifyingly effective demon-slayers.  
* **The "Lantern-Bearers":** A specialized order of who use Light Magic to guide travelers and burn away shadows in the deep woods and act as confessors and give advice and repenting instructions.

### **The Inquisition (The Grey-Wicks)**

* **Role:** The Secret Police of the Church.  
* **Mission:** To hunt down **Hedge Wizards**, **Unsanctioned Sorcerers**, **Cultists**.  
* **Tactics:** They arrest anyone  selling or offering spells and illegal charms; provide judgement immediately in the nearest settlement. They "Mark." offenders by branding marks of the eclipse on their forehead magically a sigil of bad omens everywhere and clearly marks them as not a friend of the light.,They then enslave and use them for forced labor for a set time(seem to always earn more time trying to escape). This labor is used to clear ground for new temples and an ongoing project in the mountains across the lake from the falls that is a holy endeavor and no citizens are involved. Any sign of chaos and corruption is met with witch hunting tactics.

## ---

**4.0 Theology & Relationships**

**Vs. The Moon Cult ([[Wolves]])**

* **The Relationship:** *Tense Tolerance.*  
* \*\* The View:\*\* The Church views the Moon as the "Sun’s Mirror"—a lesser, sadder light, but necessary for the night. They allow the [[Wolves]] their Moon-worship as long as the [[Wolves]] acknowledge the Sun.

**Vs. The Death Cult (Porcupines)**

* **The Relationship:** *The Necessary Shadow.*  
* **The View:** The Church preaches that the Sun brings Life, but admits someone must tidy up after Death. They view the Porcupines as "Sanctioned Undertakers." They don't sanction the spooky rituals, but they need the bodies buried.

**Vs. The Hedge Wizards**

**The Relationship:** *Active War.*

* **The View:** Hedge Wizards are fools or worse agents of chaos that subverts peoples reliance on the church(not cool). The Church hunts them relentlessly. This makes the Church unpopular in the rural borderlands where farmers rely on those wizards for charms and minor spells that help them.

## ---

**5.0 Magic Style: The Solar Arts**

Church Signature Magic is all about Blinding flashes, searing beams of heat, and "Truth-Light" that reveals invisible enemies.

* **Abjuration (Shields):** They excel at creating magical barriers to hold back the Chaos Storms.  
* **Healing:** While Porcupines use science/alchemy, Clerics use **Faith Healing**. It is faster but only heals flesh the body must rest to catch up
