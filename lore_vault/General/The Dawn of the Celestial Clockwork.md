# Before Time
  
  At first there was nothing  and then there was the primordial expanse  and from this chaos grew or just was the Prime Divinity, this great entity became aware of the infinite and the power within it, as his being or existence spread and grew it eventually it decided to focus the power to create  a focused and filtered version of its self. So through great ordeals he began to shape and mold the primordial  into great machines, and for eternity he kept making machines of what would become  the framework of all creation.as more pieces were built and his vision became clearer the primordial began to flow predictably. until the day when it was done  the final piece built and the being now weak and tired discharges itself as a sacrifice to create the spark that started it. the first gear began to spin and push its neighbors and it grew faster and absorbed the primordial and grew faster still. after ages the gears all synced up and got to speed and the clockwork reached it full operation and [[The 12 Powers]] burst forth from everywhere all at once. like a rainbow from white light through a prism.  The **Celestial Clockwork** was active, This was not merely a device but the very anchor of existence, a grand and intricate engine that stabilized the mortal plane. Its function was absolute, holding the raw, primordial chaos at bay and ensuring that the fundamental laws of reality remained constant and predictable.
