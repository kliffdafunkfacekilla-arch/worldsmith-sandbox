**FLUX (Finesse)**

- **The Power:** Flux is the fluid gap between the gears, governing vectors, permeability, and friction. It is the "Universal Solvent" of physics. A wielder of Flux sees the world as a series of loose connections that can be slipped, lubricated, or dissolved at will.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Acid):** You unbind the atomic glue of your targets, melting armor into slag or separating muscle from bone with caustic precision rather than brute force.
        
    - **Defense:** You align your atoms with the empty space in solid matter, allowing attacks to phase harmlessly through you, or you become as fluid as water to roll with any impact.
        
    - **Utility:** You can unlock any mechanical device by vibrating its tumblers, pass through solid walls, or separate mixed liquids into their base components.
