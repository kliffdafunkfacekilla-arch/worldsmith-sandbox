**OMEN (Intuition)**

- **The Power:** Omen is the Quantum Variable, the manipulation of entropy and time lines. It is the power of "What If." A wielder of Omen pulls on the threads of destiny, forcing unlikely outcomes to manifest and weaponizing the decay of time.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Necrotic):** You accelerate the entropy of a target, causing them to rot or rust in seconds as you steal their future time, or you "Jinx" them so their attacks inevitably fail.
        
    - **Defense:** You cloak yourself in probability, causing lethal blows to become "lucky misses" or jamming an enemy's gun at the exact moment they pull the trigger.
        
    - **Utility:** You can gamble with fate to force a success on a check, read the immediate future to predict danger, or speak with the echoes of the dead who linger in the timeline.
