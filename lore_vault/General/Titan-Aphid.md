# The Titan-Aphid (Sugar-Tick)
**Scientific Designation:** *Aphidoidea Maxima*
**Primary Rancher:** The [[Formicidae]] (Carpenter Ants)
**Farm Location:** The Honeydew-Halls (Inside the hollow trees)

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **Size:** The size of a large dog or sheep. They are soft-bodied, translucent green blobs with tiny legs.
* **Life Cycle:** They barely move. They latch onto the inner sap-veins of the Iron-Wood tree and drink continuously.
* **Defense:** None. They rely entirely on the Ants for protection.

## 2.0 Industrial Utility
* **Honeydew:** They process the tree sap and excrete a pure, concentrated sugar-syrup called "Honeydew."
    * **Uses:** It is the primary sweetener of the Canopy. It is fermented into alcohol (Mead), used in alchemy, and eaten raw by the Ants.
* **Wax:** As they age, they produce a hard wax coating harvested for sealing crates.

## 3.0 Temperament & Danger
* **Aggression:** Zero. They are living tanks of syrup.
* **The Risk:** None from the animal. The risk comes from the **Ant Farmers**. If you touch a Titan-Aphid, the Ants will view it as theft and dismantle you.
