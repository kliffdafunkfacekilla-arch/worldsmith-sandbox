**RATIO (Logic)**

- **The Power:** Ratio is the Algorithm, the geometric perfection and mathematical constants that govern cause and effect. A wielder of Ratio sees the world as a cascading equation of angles and probabilities, allowing them to exploit the geometry of combat.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Shock):** You channel the electrical impulses of the nervous system, frying enemy brains with logic loops, or discharging lightning that arcs perfectly between mathematically optimal targets.
        
    - **Defense:** You calculate the trajectory of incoming attacks before they happen, allowing you to sidestep with minimal effort, or create probability shields that force attacks to miss.
        
    - **Utility:** You can instantly solve any puzzle or cipher, analyze a structure to find its single weak point, or program spells to trigger only when specific conditions are met.
