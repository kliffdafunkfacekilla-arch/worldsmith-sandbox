**ORDO (Endurance)**

- **The Power:** Ordo is the lattice of the universe, the force of Stasis and Structure that opposes entropy. It is the cold logic of the crystal and the unyielding stone. A wielder of Ordo brings molecular motion to a halt, enforcing a rigid stillness upon a chaotic world.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Cold):** You drain the kinetic energy from targets, freezing them not just with ice, but by stopping their atoms entirely (Absolute Zero), turning flesh brittle and shattering it.
        
    - **Defense:** You reinforce the structural integrity of matter, turning your skin as hard as diamond or creating barriers of force that simply refuse to break.
        
    - **Utility:** You can preserve food or dead bodies indefinitely, lock doors so they physically cannot open, or create permanent structures like bridges and shelters that ignore decay.
