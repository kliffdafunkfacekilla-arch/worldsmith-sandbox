# ⚙️ Dragonstone and Aether-Tech

The remnants of the shattered cosmic clockwork—crystallized fragments imbued with primordial divine energy—serve as the technological foundation of the modern world. These fragments, known as **Dragonstone**, function as batteries holding immense reserves of raw chaos energy that bleeds from the twelve dragon prisons.

The key to harnessing this power lies in creating conduits and coils that direct the stone's volatile power. This fusion of material and engineering is known as **Aether-Tech**. These devices use precisely tuned metal coils and specialized conductors of various metals and geometries to convert chaotic energy into a usable, predictable force, such as kinetic propulsion for airships, industrial heat, or mechanical torque.

## 2.0 Field Scenarios: Tactical Energy Rerouting

In practical Aether-Tech engineering, multi-stage systems shunt and transform volatile energy sequentially through these conductors:

* **Scenario A (The Kinetic Drain):** A high-velocity **Force** impact strikes an **Osmium** dodecahedron. The metal stops the impact but melts into **Acid**. This acid is immediately shunted into **Gold** conduits, transmuting the energy into a **Shock** charge that is safely grounded.
* **Scenario B (The Pressure Displacement):** **Shock** energy is intercepted by **Silicon** tetrahedrons. The Silicon shatters into **Sonic** vibrations, which are caught by **Lead** spheres. The Lead absorbs the sound and dents into localized **Force** (physical pressure), distributing the kinetic load across the engine frame.
* **Scenario C (The Lux Flare):** Incoming **Force** is caught by **Osmium**, then shunted toward **Chromium** conductors. By allowing ambient **Arcane** energy to "overwrite" the Chromium's Radiant-stoppage threshold, the system triggers a violent displacement of light, creating a blinding **Radiant** flash to disable enemy sensors.

## 3.0 Navigational Mechanics: The Jitter-Rig

The single most important navigational tool for any airship crew is the "Aetheric Flux Attenuator," more commonly called the **Jitter-Rig**. Because ambient chaos constantly warps the atmosphere, static charts are highly unreliable. The Jitter-Rig allows navigators to read the texture of reality in real-time.

* **The Mechanism:** A raw, unsigiled piece of Dragonstone is suspended within a gimbal, surrounded by a series of tuning forks crafted from **iron, copper, and quartz**.
* **The Resonance:** As a vessel approaches or enters a Chaos Zone, the raw stone begins to vibrate or "jitter."
  * The **intensity** of the jitter reveals the danger level of the chaos zone.
  * The specific **material** (tuning fork) it resonates with reveals the *type* of chaos present (e.g., iron resonance warns of high physical force/gravity, while quartz resonance indicates psychic interference fields).

## 4.0 Tectonic Dynamics: The Meander and the Groan

Ostraka’s geography follows a **Hydraulic Model of Tensegrity** (the "Broken Eggshell"). The tectonic plates are floating fragments of a shattered sphere, held in place by high-velocity rivers of pressurized chaos energy known as **Aetheric Tension**. 

* **The Meander:** Shifting tension vectors (caused by lunar libration and celestial alignments) exert a physical **Aetheric Drag** on the plates, causing entire continents to pivot and rotate by 5 to 15 degrees.
* **The Groan:** When the plates torque and grind against one another during a rotation, they emit a low-frequency seismic resonance known as **The Groan**. If a navigator hears the Groan, all maps of the area are officially obsolete; the geography has literally shifted beneath them.
* **The 49-Day Cycle:** This rotational shifting follows a strict 49-day meander cycle, divided into eight distinct tectonic seasons:

| Season Name | Environmental Effect | Position in 49-Day Meander Cycle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Alignment** | Maximum tectonic stability; predictable trade routes. | Days 1–6 (Start of cycle) |
| **The Tension** | Aetheric Drag increases; winds become pressurized. | Days 7–12 |
| **The Pivot** | Tectonic plates begin a 5–15 degree rotation. | Days 13–18 |
| **The Groan** | Seismic resonance; maps become technically obsolete. | Days 19–24 (Mid-point) |
| **The Slack** | Aetheric Tension drops; reality-tearing increases. | Days 25–30 |
| **The Haze** | Atmospheric occlusion from siphoned vapor. | Days 31–36 |
| **Shadow Week** | Peak reality failure; global eclipse; tension vectors go slack. | Days 37–43 (The Tearing) |
| **The Snap** | Tension vectors lock; plates settle into new alignment. | Days 44–49 (End of cycle) |

## 5.0 Containment Failures: The Blowout

Aether-Tech is inherently dangerous. Because Dragonstone contains raw, unfiltered chaos, any failure in containment or design does not result in a standard combustion explosion. Instead, it triggers a **Blowout**—a localized rupture of reality itself. A Blowout causes unpredictable, physics-defying phenomena, such as local gravity reversals, the instantaneous petrification of living tissue, spatial distortion loops, or localized temporal deceleration.
