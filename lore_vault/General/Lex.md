**LEX (Willpower)**

- **The Power:** Lex is the Hierarchy, the Admin Permissions of the universe. It is the power of the Command Line. A wielder of Lex does not ask reality to change; they _tell_ it to. Their voice carries the weight of cosmic law, crushing resistance through sheer authority.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Psychic):** You speak Power Words (Stun, Kill, Kneel) that shut down the target's brain functions, or you crush their mind with the sheer pressure of your dominance.
        
    - **Defense:** You possess an Iron Mind, immune to control or fear. You can forbid specific actions ("I deny you passage"), creating psychokinetic barriers that cannot be crossed.
        
    - **Utility:** You can force the truth from a liar's throat, bind entities to magical contracts , or establish new laws within a region that all must obey.
