### Gazetteer of Ostraka: Master Archive of Locations and Points of Interest

**Archivist’s Preface:**  The record must reflect the reality: Ostraka is not a world; it is a machine on terminal life-support. As the Senior Archivist of the Order of the Clockwork, I submit this gazetteer not as a travelogue, but as a technical manual for navigating the "MacGyver Patch" that currently prevents our reality from dissolving into the void. To walk these lands is to walk upon a fractured shell under immense hydraulic pressure, held together by the very chaos that seeks to erode it. Precision is our only defense against the inevitable mechanical failure of the world.

##### 1. Introduction to Global Topography and Tensegrity

Ostraka’s physical layout is best defined by the  **"Broken Eggshell"**  model. The tectonic plates are floating fragments of a shattered sphere, held in a precarious state of  **Aetheric Tension** . This is a  **Hydraulic Model**  of geography: pressurized rivers of Chaos energy flow from the shattered moon,  **Cruorbus** , into the  **12 Sacred Groves** . These Groves function as massive vacuum pumps, drawing the energy into Dragonstone containment vessels to keep the Chaos Dragons imprisoned. The excess energy is pulled through the crust toward the central impact crater, the  **Convergence** , which acts as a "World-Drain."This creates a  **Hydrological Nightmare** ; as rifts open, our oceans risk draining directly into the super-heated mantle, causing apocalyptic steam explosions. The state of our world is defined by three technical conditions:

**The Drag:**  A physical, high-velocity current of pressurized Aetheric matter that exerts a "pull" on tectonic plates. Just as a river moves a barge, these currents physically drag continents toward the central World-Drain.

**The Meander:**  The phenomenon where Aetheric currents shift course due to lunar wobbles. When the flow vector changes, the entire continent pivots and rotates to align with the new tension line.

**The Groan:**  The low-frequency seismic resonance produced when tectonic plates torque and grind against one another during a rotation. If you hear the Groan, your maps are already obsolete.

##### 2. Port Sunder: The Friction Hub

Located at a primary geographical friction point, Port Sunder is a city of constant torque. It sits where plates rotate against one another, creating deep rifts that allow for the efficient mining of  **Dragonstone** .

###### 2.1 The Silt-Docks (Lower Tier)

The domain of the  **River Folk**  (Catfish/Dolphins) and the  **Sump-Kin** . The Sump-Kin are  **Toad Barons**  and  **Chem-Barons**  who view morality as a mere "inefficiency." They process raw materials and toxic industrial sludge, facilitating the  **Geopolitical Hypocrisy**  of the Heartland Alliance; nobles buy refined goods from the Toad Barons via River Folk middlemen to keep their own hands "clean."

###### 2.2 The Steam-Shelves (Middle Tier)

The industrial heart where  **Aether-Skiffs**  are manufactured. Engineers here rely on  **Voltaic Fleece** —harvested from high-altitude Sky-Grazers—for protection.  **Note:**  Raw fleece is effectively a bomb due to its capacitance; it must be shorn with ceramic blades and "drained" of its static charge before use. Once processed, it is the world's finest insulator against reality-warping "Blowouts."

###### 2.3 The High-Roosts (Upper Tier)

Dominated by the  **Avian Empire** , this tier handles luxury spice trade and high-stakes diplomacy. ##### 3. Excelsis Citadel: The Vertical Capital

Excelsis is the center of Avian power and "High Awareness." The city’s surveillance culture is shaped by the signature chaos of  **Magistar Opecten (The Mirror Lord)** , whose Chromium-based influence causes "The Clockwork Rot," accelerating time and space to dust.The city is governed by the  **High Judge**  of the  **Order of the Iron-Beak** . Upon the Judge's desk sits the amber-preserved eye of  **Emperor Kaelos the Evasive** . As Kaelos famously stated:  *"The truth is a blade. You do not hold it by the edge."*  The Iron-Beaks use this eye as a reminder that even the Avian biological resistance to lying can be violently overcome by "Yes/No" pressure; Kaelos lost the eye in the physical struggle to keep his beak shut during interrogation.**The Shadow-Seal Ritual**  High-stakes commerce is governed by "Shadow-Set" contracts. During  **Shadow Week** , "Ink-Wrights" perform a surgical procedure using ink mixed with  **D-Dust**  to tattoo terms onto the skin. The magic binds  *intent*  rather than grammar. If the contract is broken, the tattoo reacts violently, twisting the limb into a blackened, withered  **Shadow-Scar** . Those marked are  **Oath-Breakers** , commercially dead. Fulfilled contracts fade into  **Silver Scars** , a physical resume of honored deals.

##### 4. Peculiurus Spire: The Vault of Eternity

Headquarters of the  **Fulcrum Bank** , operated by the  **Bradypods**  (Sloth-folk). Their "Stasis Philosophy" and slow metabolism make them the only trusted species for long-term storage; they are biologically incapable of panic-selling or fleeing with deposits.**Security Protocols of the Spire:**

**Spider-Silk Canvas:**  The Spire is wrapped in "Iron-Weave," farmed from  **Weaver-Cows**  (Giant Spiders). Bradypods are the only ones who can farm them, as they move so slowly they do not trigger the spiders' predatory vibration-response.

**The Catch-Pole:**  Sloth guards use mechanical man-catcher hooks. Their unique "lock-in" claws allow them to hold an intruder indefinitely without expending muscle energy.

**The Deep Vault:**  High-value contracts are stored here, treated with alchemical moss to slow decay.

##### 5. The River-King's Shuffle: The Floating Hazard

An "Unsanctioned Neutral Zone" and mobile intelligence nexus, the Shuffle navigates toxic  **Aetheric Backwash**  to avoid tectonic Meander and land-based taxes.

**Propulsion:**  The vessel is pulled by  **Snail-Draught** —house-sized  **Abyssal Snails**  providing unstoppable torque via iron chains.

**The Enforcers:**  Security is provided by  **Hippos** , who provide  **"Institutionalized Intuition."**  They remove rivals based on the "intent" of Avian directors without the need for a technical command, maintaining the directors’ spiritual purity.**Gambler’s Guide to the Shuffle:**

**Aetherium Risks:**  Gambling with Aether-Tech currency carries the risk of localized reality-warping if the coins become unstable.

**Neutral Ground:**  High-value intelligence is the primary commodity, traded alongside Honey Wine.

##### 6. Undine's Undying Dance: The Vortex Hub

A stationary tavern located in a stable whirlpool within the  **Shifting Delta** , immune to tectonic Meander.

**Protection:**  The Dance is protected by the  **Sigil of Ice** —the brand of the  **Hearthless Ursine**  who act as innkeepers. They find a "new type of Hearth" in the service of travelers.

**Specialists:**   **Mungo**  (Mongoose-folk) act as anti-ambush specialists, utilizing hyper-reflexes to react to "Flux-Shifts" before they manifest.**House Rules of the Dance:**

**No Smoking:**  Explicitly prohibited. The Ursine bouncers, hyper-fixated on the safety and purity of the communal hearth, will expel violators immediately and physically.

**The Stare:**  Do not out-stare a Mungo; it is a dominance display.

##### 7. Index of Neighboring Sacred Groves

The geography is anchored by Groves containing Dragonstone Elementals that warp local reality.

**The Stillness (Stagus):**

**Element:**  Titanium (Endurance/Ice).

**Effect:**  An absolute zero environment where movement is inhibited by  **"Ice-Bound"**  entities enforcing total stagnation.

**The Heavy Lands (Tiraton):**

**Element:**  Osmium (Might/Gravity).

**Effect:**  Wild gravity fluctuations; travelers risk being crushed to paste by 10x compression.

**The Haunting (Termhill):**

**Element:**  Iridium (Intuition/Time).

**Effect:**  A temporal-bleed zone where past and future overlap; high risk of meeting  **Temporal Echoes** .

**The Melt (Aurgenas):**

**Element:**  Gold/Bismuth (Finesse/Change).

**Effect:**  Solid matter liquifies; the landscape rearranges itself daily.

##### 8. Final Synthesis: The Master Resource Chain

Ostraka’s economy is a "Triple-Hustle" to prevent any data-monopoly on the world’s stability.

**Dragonstone Flow:**  Shards move from tectonic rifts to the  **Steam-Shelves**  for refinement into Aether-Tech batteries.

**Aetherium Circulation:**  Forged during  **Shadow Week** , this currency flows from  **Excelsis Citadel**  to the  **Shuffle**  for intelligence and Honey Wine.

**Information Brokerage:**  Data on the Groves is the ultimate commodity, traded between the  **Grey Wardens**  and the  **Gilded Compass** .**The Three Laws of the Gazetteer:**

**The Necessity of Truth:**  In a world of Avian contracts, a lie is a physical liability.

**The Risk of Stagnation:**  Attributed to  **Magistar Aurgenas** ; stagnant value or architecture triggers  **"Flux-Rot"**  and dissolves into caustic sludge. Value must move to survive.

**The Price of Security:**  Security is not peace; it is the application of sufficient force to hold the broken pieces of the world together.
