## The Rise
Vecelo was a woman who lived only for the "now." She believed speed was divinity; if you were faster than the gods, you *were* the god. She served as the Dominion's counter-insurgency specialist, eliminating uprisings before the rebels had even finished planning them.

## The Tyranny
She experimented on her own nervous system to reach light-speed, becoming a blur of violence. Her rule was one of terror; her legions moved with preternatural swiftness. She demanded instant gratification, accelerating the consumption of resources until the land was stripped bare.

## The Corruption
In the void, motion lost its meaning. She became **The Silent Echo**, a dragon clad in lead that absorbs all sound. She moves faster than physics allows, creating sonic booms that she herself cannot hear. She is trapped in a state of perpetual acceleration.
