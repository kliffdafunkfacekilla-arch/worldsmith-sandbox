# The Formicidae (The Resonant-Kin)
**Common Name:** Carpenter Ant-Folk / The Carvers
**Scientific Designation:** *Camponotus Artifex*
**Role:** Luthiers, Master Joiners, Heavy Architects
**Home City:** **The Hollow-Halls** (A massive section of the Mother-Tree where the wood has been artistically hollowed out into a resonant cathedral).



## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **Physiology:** Massive insectoids standing 5 to 6 feet tall. They possess a polished, mahogany-colored chitin exoskeleton.
* **Limbs:** They have six limbs. The lower two are legs; the upper four are arms. The middle pair is for heavy lifting, while the upper pair is smaller and designed for delicate manipulation.
* **The Mandibles:** Their most vital tool. Their mandibles are razor-sharp but capable of microscopic precision. They can chew through Iron-Wood as easily as an apple, or shave off a layer of wood thinner than paper.
* **The Antennae:** These are not just feelers; they are vibration sensors. An Ant can touch a piece of wood and "feel" the density, the grain direction, and the flaws inside it.

## 2.0 Psychology & Culture
* **The Philosophy of "The One Piece":** They despise glue, nails, and screws. They believe that a structure is only strong if it is continuous. Their goal is to carve everything—furniture, houses, instruments—from a single, seamless block of wood.
* **The Hive-Echo:** While they are individuals, they operate on a low-level pheromonal "Hive-Net." They do not need to shout orders on a job site. One Ant starts cutting, and the others instinctively know where to brace the beam. It is a silent, terrifyingly efficient ballet of labor.
* **Perfectionism:** They are incapable of doing a "rush job." If you ask an Ant to build a chair, it will take three months, but it will support an elephant and sing when you tap it.

## 3.0 Industrial Role: The Artisan Guild
* **The Luthier Monopoly:** They create the **"Void-Lutes"** and **"Ghost-Cellos."**
    * *The Tech:* Because they can carve the *inside* of the instrument through the sound-hole using their mandibles, they create instruments with no seams. The resonance is acoustically perfect. A Formicidae violin sells for the price of a small castle.
* **Precision Joinery:** They carve the complex wooden stocks and fittings for the Tarsier firearms.
* **Puzzle-Boxes:** They create "Lock-Blocks"—wooden shipping containers with no visible keyhole, which only open if pressed in specific spots (using internal wooden tumblers).

## 4.0 Military Role: The Dismantlers
* **Style:** Siege Engineering / Anti-Structure.
* **Weaponry:**
    * **The "Jaw-Blade":** A heavy polearm mimicking their own mandibles, used to shear through armor.
    * **Acid-Pots:** Organic grenades filled with formic acid that dissolves metal hinges and locks.
* **Tactics:** They don't just kill the enemy; they delete their cover. A squad of Ants can dismantle a barricade or collapse a bridge in minutes using their mandibles and acid. They literally eat the enemy's defense.

## 5.0 Social Quirks
* **Pheromone Language:** They communicate emotion through scent.
    * *Smell of Pine/Citrus:* Happy/Agreement.
    * *Smell of Vinegar/Acid:* Angry/Aggressive.
    * *Smell of Burnt Sugar:* Panic/Danger.
* **The "Antennae-Tap":** To show affection or deep trust, they gently touch antennae with another being. It is an intimate sharing of vibration.
* **Diet:** They consume "Honeydew" (sugar-water farmed from aphids) and massive amounts of wood-pulp, which they digest with specialized gut bacteria. They are the only species that can snack on the furniture (though they consider it rude).
