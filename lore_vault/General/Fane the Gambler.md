## The Defection
A diviner for [[Magistar Termhill]]. Fane saw the coming Shattering in a vision—he saw the world ending in fire. When he tried to warn Termhill, she laughed at him. Fane realized the Magistars were too arrogant to see their own doom, so he stole a cache of Dragonstone shards and "bet" his life on joining the gods.
