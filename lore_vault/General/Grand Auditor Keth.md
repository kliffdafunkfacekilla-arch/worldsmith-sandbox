# Grand Auditor Keth

**Aliases:** The Ink-Blood, The Silent Ledger, The Zero-Point **Role:** High Director of [[Fulcrum Bank]] **Species:** Great Horned Owl (Ancient) **Location:** The Penthouse Eyrie, Spire of Accounts

> **"Do not weep to me about fairness. Fairness is a child's concept. I deal in Balance. And right now, your kingdom is geometrically unbalanced. We are foreclosing at dawn."**

## 1.0 The Entity: A Mind of Cold Calculus

Keth is older than most nations. He has served as the High Director for sixty years, a tenure defined by ruthless stability.

- **Visuals:** His plumage, once mottled brown, has turned the stark white of old parchment. He is rarely seen standing; he sits perched behind a desk carved from a single block of Obsidian.
    
- **The "Spectacles of Truth":** He wears a custom-engineered headset with four rotating lenses.
    
    - **Lens 1:** Standard magnification.
        
    - **Lens 2:** Aetheric-Spectral (Reveals magic/illusions).
        
    - **Lens 3:** Thermal (Reveals heart rate/stress in clients).
        
    - **Lens 4:** The "Dead-Lens" (Black obsidian, used to block out the world when he needs to think).
        

## 2.0 Psychology: The Ink-Blood

The Avians call him "Ink-Blood" because they believe he has surgically removed his own empathy. This is a half-truth.

- **The Philosophy:** Keth views the world as a **Math Problem**. War, famine, and happiness are variables.
    
    - If starving a village saves a city, he signs the order without a tremor.
        
    - If bankrupting a Noble House stops a civil war, he crushes them before lunch.
        
- **The Motivation:** He is terrified of **Entropy**. He remembers the stories of [[The Shattering]]. He believes the Bank is the only thing keeping the gears of reality meshed.
    

## 3.0 The Secret Burden: Keeper of the Coin

Keth is the current bearer of the **First Coin** (The Director's Secret).

- **The Solitude:** Because the Coin allows him to lie, he is the only Avian who understands the concept of _fiction_. This isolates him from his own species. He cannot explain a joke, a metaphor, or a deception to his kin without breaking their minds.
    
- **The Usage:** He rarely flips the coin. He holds it in his talon during negotiations.
    
    - _The Tactic:_ He feels the coin warm up when a client lies. He doesn't call them out; he just adjusts the interest rate up by 0.5% for every lie told.
