# Magistar Gavusrix

> **"The Cancerous Creator"**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School** | [[Vita]] (Vitality) |
| **Element** | Silver |
| **Original Title** | The Alchemist |

| **Pre-Corruption Name** | Sorel |

## The Rise
Gavusrix perverted [[The Fae]]'s knowledge of healing into a dark mastery of biomancy. He viewed life not as a gift, but as a resource to be harvested and refined. He was the chief physician of the Rituals, managing the life-force collection that kept the Magistars alive.

## The Tyranny
He sought to "purify" humanity by culling the weak and distilling the strong into elixirs. His laboratories were charnel houses of biological "optimization". He treated the population as livestock, breeding them for magical potency rather than consciousness.

## The Corruption
[[The Shattering]] granted him infinite life, but without the template of health. He became **The Quicksilver Mother**, a dragon of tarnished silver and weeping sores. She radiates a chaotic vitality that causes tumors, mutations, and uncontrollable growth in everything she flies over.
