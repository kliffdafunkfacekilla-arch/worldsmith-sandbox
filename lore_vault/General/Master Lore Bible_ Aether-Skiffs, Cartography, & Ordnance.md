##### 1. Aether-Skiff Classifications & Factional Engineering

The modern Aether-Skiff is a triumph of "MacGyver Patch" engineering, utilizing the volatile energy of Dragonstone to defy the Aetheric Drag—the physical, high-velocity current of pressurized Aetheric matter that exerts force on tectonic plates and pulls our continents toward the central World-Drain (The Convergence).

###### 1.1 Core Propulsion: The Chaos-Boiler

The heart of every vessel is the  **Aetheric-Reaction Engine** , colloquially known as the  **Chaos-Boiler** .

**Aetheric Reaction:**  The engine agitates  **Volatile Aether fluid**  using  **sigil-paste conduits**  and precisely tuned  **metal coils** . This process is notoriously unpredictable; output is affected by geographical location, lunar libration, and even the emotional state of the engineers—who are frequently described as a blend of academic geniuses and artistic madmen.

**The Jitter-Rig:**  A navigational requirement. It utilizes a raw, unsigiled Dragonstone shard surrounded by tuning forks of iron, copper, and quartz. Navigators monitor the shard’s resonance with these forks to "feel" the texture of reality and detect incoming Signature Chaos.

**Voltaic Fleece:**  Harvested from high-altitude  **Sky-Grazers** , this material is the world’s premier insulator. To prevent catastrophic Aetheric arcing, engine rooms are lined with processed (drained) fleece. It must be shorn with ceramic or obsidian blades; metallic tools cause lethal electrical discharge.

**The Blowout:**  A total containment breach is not a mere explosion. It is a leak of raw chaos that results in "physics-defying" consequences: gravity reversals, instantaneous petrification of biological tissue, and the rewriting of neural networks into senseless, infinite math.

###### 1.2 Vessel Classifications

**Cargo Caravan:**  Heavy-lift vessels optimized for the long-haul transport of spice and Dragonstone. They utilize massive Snail-Draught torque to maintain stability in high-friction Aetheric zones.

**War Skiff:**  Heavily armed combatants designed for surgical strikes. The Scute  **"Machete"**  class is the gold standard, armored in sheet metal and optimized for precision tactical maneuvers.

**Scout/Courier:**  High-velocity ships relied upon by the  **Sciurini (Glider's Roost)** . These vessels use aerodynamic resins to outrun the onset of Flux-Rot.

**Sky-Barque:**  Luxury/diplomatic vessels of the  **High-Roosts** . These ships use  **Silk-Steel**  rigging and  **Gilded Insulation**  as symbols of both status and technical safety.

**Coil-Cutter:**  Specialized engineering ships designed for high-tension Aetheric currents, featuring reinforced multi-stage conduits to shunt ambient energy.

**Deep-Diver:**  Reinforced hulls for navigating the  **Silt-Docks**  or the toxic  **Aetheric Backwash** . These require advanced filtration to prevent molecular instability from compromising the hull.

##### 2. The Cartography Market & Tectonic Navigation

In Ostraka, a map is not a record of geography; it is a perishable prediction of rotational physics.

###### 2.1 The 49-Day Meander and Map Rot

Geography is governed by the  **Hydraulic Model of Chaos Flow** . Tectonic plates pivot to align with shifting tension vectors.

**The Meander:**  Every 49 days, plates rotate between 5 and 15 degrees.

**The Groan:**  A low-frequency seismic resonance produced when tectonic plates torque. If a navigator hears the Groan, the map is obsolete.

**Map Rot:**  Terrestrial charts expire every 49 days. Navigating with "Rotten" maps leads to  **Phase-Blindness** , where vessels enter a Chaos Zone assuming it is a stable corridor. In regions like  **The Haunting (Termhill)** , this results in "Temporal Echoes," where crews meet their own past or future corpses.

**Phase-Charts:**  Perishable currency providing rotational offsets required for safe passage.

###### 2.2 The Gilded Vellum Cartel

A trilateral monopoly designed to prevent a data-monopoly over structural anchors.

**Fulcrum Academy (Surveyors):**  Operated by  **Bradypods**  using their "Long-View" and Stasis-Vault data.

**Vaneer Concord (Printers):**  Provide administrative redundancy and Ratio-based logic to mass-produce accurate charts.

**Avian Empire (Financiers):**  Control the  **Book of Omissions** . Because Avians are biologically incapable of lying, they utilize the Book to omit critical navigational truths, making their "omitted" data more dangerous than a direct falsehood.

###### 2.3 Clandestine Navigation: Smuggler’s Plates

Used by the  **Silent Current**  and  **River Folk**  to bypass tax-zones.

**Anchor-Check Protocol:**  Smugglers verify reality-stability by cross-referencing position against the resonance of the  **12 Sacred Groves** .

**Sail-Cipher:**  Smugglers utilize variable weave-density in their  **Silk-Steel**  sails to transmit Phase-Chart updates invisibly via surface-light refraction.

###### 2.4 Sailor Slang & Navigational Jargon

**"The Drag":**  The physical pull of Aetheric currents dragging continents toward the central World-Drain.

**"Shooting the Ghost":**  Navigating by the resonance of the  **Ghost Flower** . These must be kept in the pulpy internal reservoirs of  **Cactus-Kin**  to prevent them from petrifying into useless mineral matter.

**"Phase-Blind":**  A navigator out of synchronization with the Meander, risking total molecular unmaking.

##### 3. Blackstone, D-Dust, & The Evolution of Ordnance

The military powers of Ostraka have transitioned from "The Spark" (magical fluke) toward chemical warfare and the  **Blackstone Economy** .

###### 3.1 The Blackstone Economy

The  **Chiroptera (Canopy Clans)**  produce Blackstone from nitrate-rich guano.

**Munitions:**  Propellant for firearms and signal rockets.

**Counterfeiting:**  Often used to cut  **D-Dust**  (Dragonstone Dust). While D-Dust is used for high-stakes  **Shadow-Set**  tattoos (Shadow-Scars), Blackstone-based ink lacks intent-binding properties and fails to trigger a contract's curse.

###### 3.2 The Black Scale Revolution

The  **Black Scale 7**  (Scute revolutionaries) discovered "dark glass bottles"—focused chemical explosives—in the deep mines. They used these to collapse the mountain fortress of the  **Feathered Lizards** , shifting military doctrine from slave-labor to surgical, tactical strikes.

###### 3.3 Factional Firearms & Chemical Weaponry

**Crimson Corsair Shotguns:**  High-impact boarding weapons designed for clearing decks in Aetheric Backwash.

**Tarsier "Long-Eye" Jezzails:**  Precision ballistic hardware used for long-range interdiction.

**Mungo Volley-Pistols:**  High-output hardware for "The Snap"—the moment where you either kill or die.

**Needle-Cannons:**  Heavy  **Cactus-Kin**  weapons firing clusters of iron-hard spines capable of shredding biological tissue and hull canvas.
