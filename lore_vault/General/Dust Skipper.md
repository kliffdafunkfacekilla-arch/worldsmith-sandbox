**The Scavenger/Fisher: "The Dust-Skipper"**

• **Classification:** _Avis sabulo_.
 live in the desert near [[Iron Caldera]]
• **Physiology:**

    ◦ Appearance: Small, brown-and-rust colored birds with streamlined bodies and swept-back wings, resembling a Kingfisher or Tern.

    ◦ The Nictitating Shield: They possess a heavy, transparent inner eyelid (a biologically reinforced lens) that protects their eyes from sand impact.

    ◦ The Coating: Their feathers secrete a natural oil that reduces friction to near zero, allowing them to pierce the sand without drag.

• **Behavior: "Sand-Fishing"**

    ◦ The Dive: They fly low, skimming inches above the dunes. Using acute hearing, they detect the movement of Sand-Eel larvae  or Furnace-Ticks  just below the surface. They fold their wings and dive _into_ the sand like a gannet hitting water, disappearing with a "dry splash."

    ◦ The Burst: They "swim" under the sand for a few seconds, grab the worm, and burst out, shaking off the dust instantly.

• **Habitat:**

    ◦ "The Anvil Roosts": They nest exclusively on high, flat-topped volcanic rock outcrops (Mesas) where the sand cannot reach them. These rocks are often painted white with guano, serving as navigational landmarks for **Ironborn** convoys
