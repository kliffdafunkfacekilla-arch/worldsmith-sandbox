# **Flux-Rot Sludge**

**Flux-Rot Sludge** is a highly volatile, caustic, and acidic liquid byproduct of stagnant wealth and architecture, representing the physical manifestation of entropy under the influence of **Magistar Aurgenas (The Gilded Solvent)**.

### **The Law of Transactional Velocity**
- Promulgated by the Dragon of Flux, **Magistar Aurgenas**, the decree states that *Inertia is Corrosive*.
- Any wealth, physical architecture, or biological assets that remain stagnant or hoarded physically dissolve into **Flux-Rot**.
- To prevent this dissolution, populations must maintain a high **Transactional Velocity**, keeping assets and resources in constant motion.

### **Properties & Industrial Applications**
- **Elemental Property:** Aligned with the elemental domain of **Flux (Finesse)** and gold/bismuth mixtures.
- **Corrosivity:** Dissolves standard metals and structural stonework rapidly. Gold insulation is the only passive material capable of conducting the resulting charge and resisting the acid.
- **Uses:** Despite being hazardous, it is refined and managed in processing vats by *Refinery Overseers* for use as a powerful industrial solvent or vault-melter.
- **Branding Rituals:** Used by the corrupt elites of **The Cult of Toxic Liquidity** (within the Canopy Clans) in painful branding rituals to absorb caustic power.

### **Ecological & Surgical Notes**
- **Filter-Feeding Adaptations:** The subterranean exiles known as **The River Ghosts** in the Shifting Delta have biologically adapted to filter-feed on Flux-Rot seepage, recycling the surface world's stagnant waste.
- **Health Hazards:** Prolonged exposure causes physical growths and cellular anomalies on living beings. Quill-Kin surgeons utilize single-use biological scalpel blades called **Feather-Knives** (sourced from Obsidian Razor-Striders) to surgically excise these growths.
