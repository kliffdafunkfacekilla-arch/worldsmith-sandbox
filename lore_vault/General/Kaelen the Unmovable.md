## The Defection
Originally the high-executioner for [[Magistar Tiraton]]'s Legion of Might, Kaelen was a living weapon. During the Scouring, Tiraton ordered Kaelen to collapse a mountain onto a valley of fleeing civilians just to "clear the line of sight" for the artillery. Kaelen didn't just refuse; he stepped into the path of the falling stone and used his Nexus-affinity to hold the mountain up until every soul escaped.
