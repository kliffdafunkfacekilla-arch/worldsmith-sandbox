# Ghost-Teak (Float-Wood)
**Scientific Designation:** *Tectona Spiritus*
**Primary Cultivator:** The [[Sciurini]] (Gliders)
**Grow Zone:** The Crown-Layer (Dead/Petrified Trees)

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **The Plant:** These are the skeletons of massive ancient trees that died but remained standing in the upper canopy.
* **The Wood:** Over centuries, the sun bleaches the wood bone-white, and the sap evaporates, leaving vacuum pockets inside the grain.
* **Properties:** It is incredibly strong but weighs almost nothing. A child can lift a log the size of a telephone pole.

## 2.0 Industrial Utility
* **Aviation:** The primary material for building Airship gondolas and glider-frames.
* **Instruments:** Used by the Ants to make "Void-Lutes" because the hollow wood resonates perfectly.

## 3.0 Harvest & Danger
* **Harvest Method:** "Sky-Jacking." Gliders attach ropes to the dead branches, cut them loose, and "surf" the falling log down to the collection nets.
* **The Risk:** Extremely high fatality rate. If the wind catches the log wrong, it acts like a sail and drags the cutter into the sky or slams them into the trunk.
