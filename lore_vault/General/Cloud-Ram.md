

**Common Name:** The Panda-Goat, The Red Hopper **Scientific Name:** _Capra ailurus_ **Habitat:** The High Peaks (Engineer’s Mountain Range) **Role:** Primary Livestock / Textile Resource

> **"It looks like a child's toy—soft ears, red fur, cute face. Then you see it jump thirty feet up a vertical granite slab, and you realize it’s pure muscle wrapped in a blanket."** — _Summit-Walker Shepherd_

### 1.0 Biology & Physiology

A chimera-like herbivore perfectly adapted to the thin air and freezing temperatures of the high peaks.

- **Chimera Morphology:**
    
    - **The Head:** Resembles a Red Panda ([[Red Pandas]] fulgens), featuring distinct white "tear track" markings and large, intelligent eyes. It possesses curling, majestic **Ram Horns** used for mating dominance and defense.
        
    - **The Legs:** The rear legs are disproportionately long and powerful, similar to a Mountain Hare or Kangaroo, allowing for explosive vertical leaps. The front legs end in split, hardened hooves (like a Mountain Goat) designed to grip millimeter-thin ledges.
        
- **The Coat (Thermal Wool):**
    
    - It is not magical; it is simply biologically perfect. The fur is a dual-coat system. The underlayer is incredibly dense, while the outer guard hairs are hollow (similar to a Polar Bear).
        
    - **Properties:** It retains heat even when wet and is windproof. It is the only material, other than magical **[[Voltaic Fleece]]**, that allows survival on the highest peaks during **Dimfreeze** (Winter).
        

### 2.0 Economic Importance

The Cloud-Ram is the economic engine of the **Summit-Walkers**.

- **The Wool Trade:** "Summit-Red" wool is a valuable export. It is prized by the **[[River Folk]]** and **[[Heartland Alliance]]** for winter coats. It doesn't have the static danger of Sky-Grazer fleece, making it safe for commoners to wear.
    
- **Dietary Staples:** They provide a rich, fatty milk used to make "Peak-Cheese" (a hard, salty cheese that never spoils) and their meat is a staple protein.
