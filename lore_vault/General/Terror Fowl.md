# The Terror-Fowl (Murder-Chicken)
**Scientific Designation:** *Phorusrhacidae Domestica*
**Primary Rancher:** The Marsupials (Opossums)
**Farm Location:** The Hanging Roosts (Reinforced netted enclosures)

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **Size:** Flightless birds standing 7 feet tall. They look like prehistoric raptors with colorful plumage (males are bright blue/orange, females are brown/grey).
* **The Beak:** A massive, hooked beak capable of cracking a coconut (or a skull) in one bite.
* **The Legs:** Powerful, muscular legs ending in a "Sickle-Claw." They can jump 15 feet vertically.

## 2.0 Industrial Utility
* **Meat:** The primary protein source of the Canopy. The meat is lean, gamey, and tastes like a cross between turkey and steak.
* **Eggs:** Females lay one massive, blue-speckled egg per week. One egg can feed a family of four. The shells are so thick they are used as bowls.
* **Feathers:** The "Iron-Quills" on the wings are stiff and waterproof. They are exported to fletchers for arrows and to the [[Avian Empire]] for quill-pens.

## 3.0 Temperament & Danger
* **Aggression:** Extreme. They are pack hunters. Even "domesticated" ones will swarm a farmer if they smell blood.
* **Handling:** Ranchers wear heavy plate-greaves (leg armor) to protect against the sickle-claws. Feeding time is a chaotic frenzy known as "The Snap."
