**The Divine Sacrifice and The Engineer**

  

To prevent the total unmaking of the mortal plane, the surviving elder gods enacted a desperate final gambit by **dissolving their physical forms** to create a **divine containment system**. Their dissolved essence provided the energy for the ley lines and the global network of prisons.

**The Thirteenth: Sh’lar, The Prime Corruptor**

  

The 13th Magister was **Sh’lar**, a Fae representative who betrayed her people to aid the Magistars. Unlike the others, her brand is **Subtle Entrenchment**. Instead of direct destruction, she focused on **corrupting the divine seals** from within, ensuring the other dragons' influence would eternally bleed into the world.


  
  
  

The youngest deity, **The Engineer**, was charged with the solitary final task of "setting the stones". He gathered the dragons and anchored them into the **12 Sacred Groves**—the magically potent sites of [[The Fae]] Elders' earlier sacrifice. The **First Wardens** were a handful of surviving human **Magistars** who fought alongside the gods during [[The Shattering]]. Following the **[[The Divine Sacrifice]] **, they were magically **oath-bound to the Engineer** god to preserve the global prison system and contain the influence of the Chaos Dragons.

  

**Origins and Early History**

  

For centuries, [[History/The First Wardens]] lived within the petrifying remains of the Engineer, studying the **[[Convergence]]** and the seeping corruption of the Magistars-turned-dragons. Their internal ranks eventually fractured due to a brutal **civil war** against corrupted members. Realizing their immortality was broken and their numbers were dwindling, the three remaining survivors divided the labor of ensuring a permanent legacy of guardianship.

  

**[[The Three Foundational Leaders]]**
