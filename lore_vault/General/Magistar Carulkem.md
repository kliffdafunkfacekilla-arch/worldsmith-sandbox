## The Rise
Carulkem was the spiritual core of the hatred against [[The Fae]]. He believed that a lie told with enough conviction becomes the truth. He was the master of propaganda, using magical displays to fuel the populace's fear and cement the narrative that their suffering was a holy necessity.

## The Tyranny
He enforced a state of constant war-readiness. He stripped the "truth" and identity out of his subjects, replacing their history with the Magistars' fabrication. His fortitude was not for protection, but for the endurance of hate.

## The Corruption
His burning hatred ignited his physical form. He became **The Furnace Eternal**, a dragon of red-hot tungsten coils. He triggers "Elemental Catastrophe," turning the world around him into an oven. He is a creature of pure thermal rage, forever burning but never consumed.
