## The Defection
A high-ranking blood-alchemist for [[Magistar Gavusrix]]. She was tasked with "harvesting" the essence of [[The Fae]]. Instead of refining the essence for Gavusrix’s longevity, she secretly used it to create a biological "anti-virus" that protected the early rebel camps from the Magistars' plagues.
