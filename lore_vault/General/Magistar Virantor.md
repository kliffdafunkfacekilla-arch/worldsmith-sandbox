# Magistar Virantor

> **"The Voice of Ruin"**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School** | [[Aura]] (Charm) |
| **Element** | Phosphorus |
| **Original Title** | The Golden Voice |
| **Dragon Form** | [[The Burning Soul]] |
| **Pre-Corruption Name** | Malaki |

## The Rise
Virantor believed that if you could make people love their chains, the chains didn't exist. He was the public face of the Magistars, using magical voice projection to sway large crowds and turn near-rebellion into mass fervor.

## The Tyranny
He was the ultimate deceiver. He eroded the morals of the populace, convincing them that slavery was "divine service" and that [[The Fae]] were demons. He fed on the adoration of the mob, leading them to their deaths with a smile.

## The Corruption
His charm became a radioactive toxicity. He became **The Burning Soul**, a dragon composed of burning Phosphorus smoke. His presence is no longer charismatic but intoxicatingly lethal; his "voice" burns the souls of those who hear it, turning them into "The Golden Tyrant's" mindless worshippers.
