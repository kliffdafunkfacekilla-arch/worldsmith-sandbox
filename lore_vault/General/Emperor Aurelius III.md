# Emperor Aurelius III ("The Prism")

> **"Do not bore me with the gray details of the tax code. Make the numbers sing, or I will find a Treasurer who can."**

## 1.0 Overview: The Weapon of Vanity
Aurelius III is the current Emperor of the **[[Avian Empire]]**. While his predecessors ruled through evasion or military might, Aurelius rules through **Aesthetic Domination**. He believes that if the Empire looks flawed, it *is* flawed.

* **Species:** Albino Peacock (A rare genetic anomaly).
* **Visuals:** He is blinding to look at. He wears robes of pure white **[[Avian Silk]]** woven with gold thread.
* **The Crown:** He does not wear a metal crown; he *is* the crown. When he fans his massive tail feathers during Senate hearings, the eyespots are dyed with luminescent alchemical ink, creating a wall of "watching eyes" that physically illuminates the chamber.

## 2.0 Psychology & Tactics
Aurelius uses his beauty as a bludgeon.
* **The "Wall of Light":** In negotiations, he fans his tail to physically block the view of his opponents, forcing them to look only at him. It is a disorientation tactic—it is hard to negotiate trade tariffs when you are staring into a wall of glowing eyes.
* **The Obsession:** He is currently obsessed with "Geometric Purity." He has ordered the **[[Roost Building Guild]]** to re-carve the entrance of the Capital to align perfectly with the sunrise on the Equinox, a project that is driving the **[[Beavers]]** (contractors) insane.
