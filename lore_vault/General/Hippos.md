# The Hippos 

**Aliases:** The Velvet-Skins, The Proxy-Lords, The Wide-Mouths **Native Region:** The Opal-Delta (Bordering [[Canopy Clans]] and Swamp) **Role:** Bank Managers, Heavy Security, Intermediaries

> **"You want to speak to the Bird? No. The Bird is busy flying. You speak to me. I am the rock the bird lands on. And right now, the rock says your credit is no good."** — _Branch Manager "Heavy" Horgus_

## 1.0 Biology: The Deceptive Tanks

Hippos are often mistaken for fat, lazy hedonists. This is a fatal error.

- **Physiology:** They are biological siege engines. Beneath the layer of subcutaneous fat is muscle density comparable to an **Ursine**.
    
    - **The Maw:** Their jaw strength can snap a boat oar or a  femur like a twig.
        
    - **The "Velvet" Skin:** Their skin is incredibly sensitive to dryness. They must moisturize constantly. This biological need drives their obsession with **Baths** and **Spas**.
        
- **The "Yawn":** When a Hippo yawns, displaying their tusks, it is not fatigue. It is a **Threat Display**. It means "I am charging in three seconds."
    

## 2.0 The Culture of the Proxy (The Interface)

The Hippos occupy a unique niche: **They are the translators of Intent.**

- **The Problem:** Avians cannot do "dirty business" (Black Market, Smuggling, Intimidation) because they cannot lie or deal with "unclean" souls.
    
- **The Solution:** The Hippo Interface.
    
    - **High EQ:** Hippos possess terrifyingly high Emotional Intelligence. They read micro-expressions, tone, and body language better than any species.
        
    - **The "Ear-Flick" Language:** Hippos have a secret, non-verbal sign language using their small, dextrous ears and grunts.
        
        - _Scenario:_ An Avian Director sits with a Hippo Manager and a Pirate Captain. The Avian says, "We hope for a resolution." The Avian's intent is violence. The Hippo sees the Avian's pupil dilate. The Hippo flicks his left ear twice. The Hippo guards behind the Pirate lock the door. The Avian never gave an order.
            

## 3.0 The Bathhouse Economy

Hippo culture revolves around water.

- **The Office:** Hippos do not like desks. They do business in **Hot Springs** and **Bathhouses**.
    
- **The "Naked Truth":** Conducting a meeting in a bathhouse is a strategic power move.
    
    - **No Weapons:** You cannot hide a dagger in a towel.
        
    - **Vulnerability:** Everyone is equal in the steam.
        
    - **The Heat:** Hippos love boiling heat; mammals and reptiles struggle. A Hippo Manager will crank the heat up until the client passes out or agrees to the deal.
        

## 4.0 Opal-Wallow: The Retirement City

Located on the edge of the Great Lake, this is the "Vegas" of the Hippo world.

- **The Vibe:** Lazy luxury. Lazy River with bar sevice, iced drinks, endless buffets, and high-stakes gambling.
    
- **The Paddle-Wheel Casino:** _The River-King's Shuffle_.
    
    - A massive, gilded boat that never docks. It is neutral territory.
        
    - **The Game:** "Tiles." A complex domino-like game that Hippos excel at because it requires reading the opponent's face, not just the math.
        
- **The Retirement Plan:** Every Hippo Bank Manager dreams of saving enough Aetherium to buy a "Mud-Villa" in Opal-Wallow and spend their final years playing Tiles and eating spice-dusted marsh-melons.
