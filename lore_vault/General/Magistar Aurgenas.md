## The Rise
Aurgenas was a master of surgical precision and political poison. He consolidated power not by armies, but by infiltration. He commanded the Dominion's spy networks, delighting in the "elegance" of his betrayals and believing that controlling information made him a god.

## The Tyranny
He manipulated trade and magical flow to pit ambitious underlings against one another. Under his rule, trust was eradicated; families turned on families for a handful of coin or a scrap of safety. He turned the economy into a weapon, where wealth was the only morality.

## The Corruption
[[The Shattering]] melted his form along with his greed. He became **The Gilded Solvent**, a dragon of molten gold. But the gold is a lie—it is a corrosive acid that dissolves everything it touches. He is a creature of constant, painful shifting, forever melting and reforming.
