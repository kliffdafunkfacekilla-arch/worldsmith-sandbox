**LUX (Awareness)**

- **The Power:** Lux is the Observer Effect, the power of light and perception. By observing the universe, the wielder collapses the wave function and defines truth. A wielder of Lux commands the photon, revealing what is hidden and hiding what is plain.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Radiant):** You condense photons into "Hard Light" lasers that sear through matter, or unleash flashes of brilliance that permanently blind enemies and halt regeneration.
        
    - **Defense:** You bend light around you to become invisible, or project holographic decoys (illusions) to confuse the enemy's targeting.
        
    - **Utility:** You possess "True Sight," seeing through walls, illusions, and darkness. You can project your vision to scry on distant locations or illuminate the deepest shadows.
