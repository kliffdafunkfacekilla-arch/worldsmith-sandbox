#  The Simians (The High-Speed Traders)

**Common Name:** Monkey-Folk / The Golden Horde

**Scientific Designation:** _Simia Mercator_

**Role:** Bankers, Harvesters, Grenadiers

**Home City:** **Simia-Reach** (A vertical metropolis of ropes, swing-bridges, and open-air markets).

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance

- **Physiology:** The Simians are highly dimorphic, split into two distinct biological sub-castes that work in tandem.
    
    - **The Tamarins (The Elite):** Small (3-4ft), wiry, with lustrous golden or orange fur. They have long, nimble fingers perfect for counting coins and picking locks.
        
    - **The Howlers (The Labor):** Larger (5-6ft), broad-shouldered, with dark fur and imposing throat-sacs. They handle the heavy crates and the security.
        
- **The Tail:** Fully prehensile. It acts as a third arm. A Simian trader can sign a contract with their right hand, hold a cup of coffee with their left, and hang from a branch with their tail—all simultaneously.
    
- **Agility:** They possess "brachiation reflexes." They are uncomfortable on flat ground. They are biologically wired to calculate trajectory and distance instantly, making them natural acrobats.
    

## 2.0 Psychology & Culture

- **The Philosophy of "Flow":** To a Simian, stillness is death. They believe in _Kinetic Capital_. Money must be spent, goods must be shipped, and bodies must be moving. A quiet bank is a failing bank.
    
- **Risk Tolerance:** They are adrenaline junkies. They harvest fruits from the thinnest, most wind-swept branches because that’s where the best produce grows. They view "Safety" as a concept for Sloths and Opossums.
    
- **Communication:** They speak fast and loud.
    
    - **"The Click-Math":** When calculating numbers, they click their tongues rapidly against the roof of their mouths. A busy trading floor sounds like a swarm of cicadas.
        
    - **"The Howl":** The larger Howlers use low-frequency bellows to signal market openings or warn of danger across miles of jungle.
        

## 3.0 Industrial Role: The Agro-Bankers

- **The Vertical Farms:** They manage the **Sun-Melon**, **Iron-Pear**, and **Shadow-Truffle** plantations. This is dangerous work; the best fruit grows at the very top of the canopy (where birds of prey hunt) or deep in the roots (where the rot is).
    
- **The Sky-Banks:** Because they harvest the cash crops, they also manage the loans. They lend money to the other clans to buy their equipment.
    
    - _The Interest Rate:_ Simian loans are short-term with high interest. They expect a return _fast_.
        

## 4.0 Military Role: The Skirmishers

- **Style:** Guerrilla Warfare / 3D Combat.
    
- **Weaponry:**
    
    - **Thrown Weapons:** They are the best throwers in the Tangle. They use **Chakrams** (circular blades) and **Javelins**.
        
    - **Grenadiers:** They are the primary users of the **Bat-Clan's Explosives**. A Simian can swing from a vine, drop a "Canopy-Cracker" into an enemy formation, and swing away before it detonates.
        
- **Tactics:** "Hit and Run." They never hold a line. They attack from above, confuse the enemy with noise and smoke, and vanish.
    

## 5.0 Social Quirks

- **Grooming as Currency:** Picking bugs or dirt out of someone’s fur is a sign of intense respect and business trust. If a Bank Manager starts grooming your shoulder during a meeting, it means the loan is approved.
    
- **Fashion:** They love noise. They wear vests covered in bells, chimes, and loose coins. A wealthy Simian jingles when they walk. Silence is for the poor.
    
- **Diet:** High-sugar. They consume massive amounts of fruit and **Shadow-Chocolate** to fuel their hyper-active metabolisms. They suffer from "Sugar-Crashes" where they become suddenly lethargic and irritable.
