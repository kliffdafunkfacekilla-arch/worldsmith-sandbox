# Magistar Termhill

> **"The Blind Prophet"**

## The Rise
Termhill was obsessed with the "Weak Point" in reality. She believed she could find the single thread that, if pulled, would unmake the gods. She acted as the Dominion’s chief diviner, predicting weaknesses in [[The Fae]]'s barrier and eliminating emerging heroes before they could rise.

## The Tyranny
She ruled through pre-emptive punishment. Citizens were executed for crimes they had not yet committed. She ignored every omen of her own destruction, blinded by the arrogance that she could outmaneuver fate itself.

## The Corruption
Fate snapped back. She became **The Black Star**, a dragon of Iridium and shadow. She is "The Shifting Web," weaving illusions of strategic ruin. She exists in a state of "Temporal Instability," tearing open time rifts and inflicting necrotic decay on the future itself.
