**NEXUS (Fortitude)**

- **The Power:** Nexus governs the atomic bonds and the state of matter (Solid, Liquid, Gas, Plasma). It is the industrial forge of the cosmos. A wielder of Nexus controls the excitement level of atoms, turning the passive world into a volatile chemical reaction.
    
- **Functions:**
    
    - **Offense (Fire):** You excite the atoms of the air until they combust into superheated plasma, vaporizing enemies or melting through steel bulkheads with concentrated thermal lances.
        
    - **Defense:** You ground yourself against energy, absorbing heat or shock, or you transmute your skin into steel or stone to ignore physical trauma.
        
    - **Utility:** You are the ultimate crafter, able to fuse broken items back together, transmute base materials (turning mud to stone), or reshape raw matter into weapons and tools.
