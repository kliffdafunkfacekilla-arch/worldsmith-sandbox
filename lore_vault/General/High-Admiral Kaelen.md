# High-Admiral Kaelen ("The Golden Talon")

> **"You have three seconds to alter your course. One. Two. Fire."**

## 1.0 Command Profile
The Supreme Commander of the Imperial Fleet and Captain of the flagship, **[[The Radiant Raptor]]**. [[Kaelen the Unmovable]] represents the "Martial Truth" of the Empire—direct, overwhelming, and indisputable.

* **Species:** Golden Eagle.
* **Visuals:** He is physically imposing, with a wingspan that dominates any room. He wears a breastplate of polished **Aetherium** (lightweight gold alloy) and drapes his wings in a heavy cape of **[[Voltaic Fleece]]** to signal his immunity to lightning/shock tactics.

## 2.0 The "Unblinking Stare"
[[Kaelen the Unmovable]]’s signature tactic is silence.
* **The Method:** He invites enemy captains aboard for parley and simply stares at them. Because Avians cannot lie, his silence is terrifying. It implies he has nothing to discuss because their defeat is already a mathematical fact.
* **Notable Victory:** The "Silent Siege of the Western Drift." He stared down a **[[Crimson Corsairs]]** Lord for twenty minutes until the Corsair, convinced [[Kaelen the Unmovable]] had a trap he couldn't see, retreated without a shot fired.
