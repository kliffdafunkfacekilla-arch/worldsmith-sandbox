**⚔️ Faction Dossier: The Wolverine Brotherhood**

*(The Unmatched Guardians)*

**"We serve the Duty, not the Man."**

#### **1.0 Overview: The Independent Shield**

The Wolverines are not merely soldiers; they are an independent martial order. While they serve as the elite guards for both the **[[Ursine Hegemony]]** and the **[[Heartland Alliance]]**, they maintain total autonomy. They are politically indifferent, incapable of treachery, and frighteningly efficient.

#### **2.0 Psychology & Behavior: The Stoic Wall**

* **The "Bad" Bodyguard:** They are notoriously difficult for VIPs to manage. A Wolverine will protect your life with absolute ferocity, but they will **not obey your orders**. If a King orders his Wolverine guard to "open the door," the Wolverine will ignore him if opening the door compromises security. They can (and will) physically block their own charges from entering unsafe areas.  
* **Indifferent Loyalty:** They cannot be bribed or seduced because they don't care about politics. They serve the *contract*, not the *cause*.  
* **Command Structure:** They only recognize military rank from **Bear** (Ursine) or **Wolf** (Heartland) commanders. They will defend a Deer politician to the death, but they will not take orders from them.

#### **3.0 Tactics: The Art of Misdirection**

They are undefeated as guards ("No one has gotten past them to date that is known of"). This success comes from deception, not just strength.

* **The Empty Room:** A classic Wolverine tactic is to heavily guard an empty, armored room to draw assassins, while the actual VIP is sitting in a non-descript janitor’s closet with a single, hidden guard.  
* **Patrol Mimicry:** They utilize "decoy patrols" that follow obvious, loud routes to misdirect intruders away from the real perimeter.

#### **4.0 The Mystery of the "Hidden Town"**

* **Location:** A rugged, rocky woodland on the tri-border of the Ursine, Heartland, and Sylvan territories (near [[Iron Caldera]]/Vaneer border).  
* **The Deal:** They have a treaty with the major powers: *No one builds in our woods, and we will serve as your elite guards for basic soldier's wages.*  
* **The Anomaly:** They only have **one visible town**, which is far too small to house their known numbers.  
* **The Secret:** They negotiated a strict **"No-Fly Zone"** over their territory. Even the **[[Avian Empire]]** and **Sky-Barons** respect it. This implies that the bulk of their civilization—and their brutal training grounds—is hidden, possibly underground or camouflaged.

#### **5.0 Visuals & Gear**

Their appearance is distinct and terrifying.

* **Combat Gear:**  
  * Straight, angular-cut **Black Light Leather** armor.  
  * Reinforced with **Blackened Chainmail** at the joints and vitals.  
  * Weapons: They are deadly with anything they pick up, but are considered lethal even unarmed.  
* **Ceremonial/Guard Uniform (The "Bleeding" Look):**  
  * **Torso:** Cross-bandoliers of blood-red fabric with white accents.  
  * **Lower Body:** A matching kilt-like covering that reaches mid-leg.  
  * **Arms:** Gauntlets from wrist to elbow with a vicious, elongated **"Elbow Spike"** for close-quarters striking.  
  * **Feet:** Unique footwear that covers only the *top* of the foot (perhaps for better traction/claw use).  
* **The "Mohawk" Muzzle Plate:**  
  * The most iconic piece of their kit. A leather armor plate protects the muzzle (nose to eyes).  
  * Straps pull back around the head, holding **long, multi-colored leather strings**.  
  * **Effect:** When they move or fight, these strings sway and snap, creating the visual silhouette of a chaotic, colorful mohawk or mane.
