# Master Archivist's Chronicle: The Metaphysical Schools

> **Archivist's Journal - Clary's Personal Notes:**
> *"To touch the mind, to bend the law, to read the future—these are the metaphysical arts. Let us examine the schools of Lex, Lux, Ratio, Anumis, Aura, and Omen, and the brave Nulls who stand untouched by their currents..."*

---

## Anumis

---
dg-publish: true
---
# ANUMIS (The Unmaking of the Pattern)

Anumis is the manipulation of the primordial tapestry of creation. Masters of this art do not conjure physical elements; they reach into the invisible, geometric lattice of the world and temporarily unmake the structural truth of an object. They can force reality to behave contrary to its nature—causing a wagon wheel to refuse to roll, or a solid shield to forget it is meant to repel a blade. Defensively, they re-weave their local pattern so that incoming attacks hit the space where the tapestry dictates they "aren't". When Anumis is wielded, the air ripples like a severe heat mirage, accompanied by a deep, sickening hum of unmaking that vibrates in the teeth of anyone nearby. The wielders themselves are profoundly unsettling; their eyes constantly dart as if reading invisible text woven into the air. Their fingers are often permanently stained with ethereal, glowing ink that never washes clean, and they tend to speak in disjointed, unnerving truths, answering questions before they are even asked.

## Ratio1

---
dg-publish: true
---
# RATIO (The Galvanic Lattice)

Ratio governs the symmetry and equivalent exchange of reality. By manipulating the unseen galvanic currents that connect all things, a wielder creates massive, unnatural imbalances in the aether. When reality violently snaps back into place to restore the balance, it unleashes devastating chain-lightning and shocking force. A master can cause a target's physical form to violently stutter and glitch, defying all natural logic, or introduce a "glitch" into an incoming attack so a blade erratically misbehaves before striking. The manifestation of Ratio is heralded by the overwhelming scent of burnt ozone and a sharp, deafening crack of tearing static that makes the hair on the back of the neck stand on end. Consequently, Ratio wielders are hyper-alert and twitchy. Their hair is permanently frizzed from ambient static, and touching their skin often results in a painful shock. They rarely blink, processing the geometry of a room with an intense, unyielding stare.

## Lex

---
dg-publish: true
---
# LEX (The Divine Mandate)

Lex is the crushing weight of cosmic law. It is the power to command reality through sheer, unyielding spiritual authority. A wielder of Lex does not ask the world to change; they speak unquestionable mandates that a target's body must obey out of fundamental, existential awe. They can establish localized moral laws—such as declaring a space immune to drawn blades—that reality accepts as undeniable fact. When Lex is invoked, a crushing, oppressive weight fills the air, mimicking the heavy silence before a thunderclap. The wielder's voice echoes with a multi-layered, terrifying resonance that physically presses against the eardrums. Those who master Lex exhibit an unnatural, perfect posture and are physically incapable of whispering. Their gaze is so dominant that common folk instinctively look down when they pass.

## Omen1

---
dg-publish: true
---
# OMEN (The Inevitable Rot)

Omen is the weaponization of the inevitable river of time and entropy. Wielders pull upon the threads of destiny to accelerate decay, causing enemies and fortifications to wither, rust, and turn to dust in seconds as their inevitable end catches up to them all at once. Defensively, they can step slightly out of sync with the present, allowing lethal blows to pass harmlessly through the space they occupied a fraction of a second prior. The use of Omen fills the air with the smell of dry dust, old tombs, and autumn leaves, accompanied by a sudden, chilling sensation of profound sorrow. The cost of this power is high; Omen wielders age visibly when channeling their magic. Their shadows often detach or lag slightly behind their physical movements, and mechanical clocks or ticking gears will always seize and stop when they enter a room.

## Aura

---
dg-publish: true
---
# AURA (The Emotional Resonance)

Aura is the raw, gravitational pull of the soul. A wielder projects emotion so intense it shatters reason, inflicting the profound sadness that breaks the spirit, the rush of addiction that cripples the mind, or a love that burns so hot it destroys a target from the inside out. They can wrap themselves in absolute awe or despair, making it emotionally impossible for enemies to raise a hand against them. The manifestation of Aura is accompanied by a sudden, overpowering scent of nostalgic memories—such as rain on cobblestones or a lost lover's perfume—while the hearts of those nearby either race frantically or drop into absolute dread. Wielders possess impossibly captivating, dilated eyes. Unconsciously, people around an Aura master will begin to mirror their breathing and posture, hanging on their every word with desperate devotion.

## Lux

---
dg-publish: true
---
# LUX (The Revealing Glare)

Lux is the absolute, undeniable truth of perception. Wielders unleash searing waves of heatless energy that cross distances instantly, burning targets with the raw, blinding truth of creation, or trapping them in inescapable, terrifying mirages. They can manipulate the necessity of perception, bending light to become completely unseen. The use of Lux creates a high-pitched, ringing silence. The air becomes starkly defined, and all natural shadows are violently erased by a searing brilliance. The skin of a Lux wielder becomes faintly translucent over time, and their eyes reflect light like cut glass, glowing faintly even in pitch darkness. Terrifyingly, the most powerful masters of Lux cast no shadow at all.

## The Nulls

1. The Anatomy of Untethering: Absence of the Spark

Within the mechanical hierarchy of the Shattered World, the population is divided by a fundamental interface capability. While approximately 50% of the populace possesses some degree of innate connection to the twelve schools of magic, the remaining 50% are categorized by the [[Order of the Clockwork]] as "The Nulls," or the Untethered. To an Archivist, these individuals represent a biological failure to manifest the "Spark" mentioned in the vernacular of the Horned Commons.Clinical analysis reveals a total void where the school of **[[Vita]] (Vitality)** should serve as a biological anchor to reality's twelve primary schools. Because they lack a biological Aetheric Tether to interact with the The Clockwork Lattice, Nulls are uniquely characterized by an inability to perceive "The Hum"—the planetary siren song of [[Magistar Virantor]]. Without the metaphysical hardware of **[[Lex]] (Willpower)** or **[[Aura]] (Influence)** , they are structurally immune to the euphoric fanatical compulsions that plague the Attuned. This absence necessitates specific psychological adaptations:

- **Material Basicness:** A spartan, subterranean-tier stoicism (derivative of the [[Reliance]] philosophy) prioritizing the mastery of the body over the [[Reliance]] on external "miracles."
- **Unyielding Stubbornness:** A rigid internal structural integrity (forged in the spirit of the [[Iron Caldera]]) required to survive in a reality that refuses to acknowledge their existence.

2. The Clockwork Lens: Perceiving the The Clockwork Lattice

While the Attuned interact with reality through instinctual "feel," the Null perceives the underlying **[[Anumis]]-structures** through a purely clinical lens. To the Untethered, the universe is not a tapestry of wonder, but a "Celestial Clockwork" that can be deciphered and manipulated via the Navigator’s Art."Life is not a miracle; it is a math problem to be balanced. The universe possesses structural keys; we merely need the correct tools to read the patterns and execute [[Magistar Metrion]]'s zero-sum logic." — _Magistar Metrion, [[Magistar Metrion]] of Zero_Lacking the **[[Lux]] (Awareness)** or **Omen (Intuition)** to see magic directly, the Null utilizes a **Jitter-Rig** as their primary sensory proxy. This device—an unsigiled Dragonstone suspended within a gimbal and surrounded by iron, copper, and quartz tuning forks—translates the vibration and intensity of reality into mechanical feedback. By reading the specific resonance of the forks, a Null can "feel the texture of reality," identifying incoming chaos surges or atmospheric instabilities before they manifest.

3. Mechanics of Defiance: Tool-Based Reality Manipulation

The Untethered employ **[[Aether-Tech]]** to bypass the biological limitations of their species. At the core of this defiance is the **Closed-Loop Aetheric-Drive (Metaphysical Centrifuge)**. Unlike the Attuned, whose volatile emotions can cause engine instability, the Null’s inherent emotional stability acts as a safety feature, preventing the unpredictable output often seen in "Spark-heavy" engineers.Nulls utilize specific metals to manipulate the The Clockwork Lattice’s variables:

| Metal | Functional Role in Null Tools | The Clockwork Lattice Property |
| ------ | ------ | ------ |
| **Lithium** | **Willpower Hardening:** Absorbs spirit to release willpower. | Commands Lex |
| **Osmium** | **Gravity Anchoring:** Absorbs willpower to release physical force/gravity. | Commands Mass |
| **Tungsten** | **Thermal Ignition:** Absorbs force to release extreme heat/plasma. | Commands Nexus |
| **Gold** | **Fluid Dissolution:** Absorbs heat/fire to release acid/adaptability. | Commands Flux |
| **Silver** | **Biological Spark:** Absorbs acid to release poison/cellular life. | Commands Vita |
| **Lead** | **Kinetic Velocity:** Absorbs poison to release sonic motion/velocity. | Commands Motus |
| **Iridium** | **Temporal Decay:** Absorbs sonic vibration to release necrotic entropy. | Commands Omen |
| **Titanium** | **Frozen Stasis:** Absorbs necrotic decay to release cold/stillness. | Commands Ordo |
| **Silicon** | **Electrical Logic:** Absorbs cold to release shock/geometry. | Commands Ratio |
| **Bismuth** | **Arcane Unravelling:** Absorbs shock to release arcane knowledge/truth. | Commands Anumis |
| **Chromium** | **Radiant Perception:** Absorbs arcane to release light/perception. | Commands Lux |
| **Phosphorus** | **Spiritual Resonance:** Absorbs light to release spirit/harmony. | Commands Aura |

The inherent risk of forcing effects on reality via sigil-paste conduits is the **"Blowout."** When a tool fails, raw chaos leaks, resulting in localized reality-warping. These events manifest as localized gravity flipping, the instantaneous petrification of biological tissue, or the horrific transformation of living flesh into inanimate metal.

4. The Pariah State: Hunted and Socially Eroded

The Untethered exist as "static" in a world of high-frequency social cohesion. They are subjected to systemic erosion by the major powers:

1. **Biological Purges:** In the [[Hive Commonwealth]], larvae exhibiting a disconnected "Rogue" status from the collective senses are typically destroyed to maintain the hive-mind’s integrity.
2. **Social Death:** Ursine society brands Nulls with the **Sigil of Ice** , declaring them **"[[Hearthless]]."** This status is an erasure of identity, rendering the individual non-existent to the community.
3. **Inquisitorial Pursuit:** Church Inquisitors hunt "Hedge-Wizards"—Nulls who use unauthorized tools to mimic clergy-sanctioned miracles.
4. **The Curse of the Breach:** Null traders are checked by **"Shadow-Set"** contracts. While a breach causes a limb to wither and blacken, a Null who honors a thousand high-stakes deals is recognized by the **"Silver Scars"** covering their skin—a physical resume of integrity.Despite this, Nulls often find tactical kinship with the **Wolverine Brotherhood** . They respect the Brotherhood’s "Duty over Man" philosophy and have adapted the [[Wolverines]]' misdirection tactics, such as the use of decoy patrols, to evade magically-attuned predators.

5. Notable Archetypes and Tactical Protocols

Nulls have evolved specialized roles to maximize their mechanical expertise:

- **Aether-Wrights (Sigil-Wrights):** The "artistic madmen" of the forge who balance the closed-loop cosmic circuit of the Aether-Drive.
- **The Grounded ([[Storm-Lancers]]):** Scute veterans who have outlived their bonded Cloud-Cutter Rays. Having lost their "Wingman" connection, they serve as mentors, possessing a profound understanding of flight mechanics and the tragic "Red Ghost" phase, where a Ray goes berserk upon its rider's death.
- **Outcast Traders:** Strategic middlemen who facilitate the conversion of hive resources (Quartz crystals and Honey Wine) into external capital.To navigate hostile territory, Nulls employ the **"Silent-Ride"** and the **"Trinity of Silence"** —a protocol adapted from the Cactus, Yucca, and Tumbleweed kin. By relying on non-verbal **Thump-Codes** and sensory feedback (vibration, motion, and wind), Nulls operate with machine-like efficiency, bypassing the need for telepathy or "The Hum" to remain undetected.

6. Summary: The Economy of the Now

Adhering to the pragmatism of the [[Eastern Hounds]], the Untethered reject the "weaponized past" used by Magistars to exert control. Their existence is governed by the **Untethered Doctrine** :

- **The Now:** Life must be lived in the absolute present. The past is a failure of data; the future is an uncalculated variable.
- **Fair Game:** No resource is owned by divine right. If a resource, such as a **Snail-Barge** , cannot be maintained through engineering and will, it is "fair game" for culling and reclamation.
- **Voluntary Amnesia:** The systematic use of **Grave-Berries** (The Oblivion-Fruit) to suppress long-term trauma and historical burdens, allowing the Null to focus purely on the mechanical requirements of survival.
