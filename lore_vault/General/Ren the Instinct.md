## The Defection
The youngest captain, serving under [[Magistar Vecelo]]. During a "training exercise," Vecelo ordered Ren to slaughter his own squad to test his reaction speeds. In a blur of motion, Ren didn't kill his men; he disarmed the Magistar herself and used the distraction to lead his entire unit into the service of the Elder Gods.
