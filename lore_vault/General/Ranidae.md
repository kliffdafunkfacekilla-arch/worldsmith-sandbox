# The Ranidae (The Alchemists)
**Common Name:** Frog-Folk / The Rain-Callers
**Scientific Designation:** *Rana Venenum Sapiens*
**Role:** Apothecaries, Poisoners, Chemical Engineers
**Home City:** **Tox-Hollow** (A collection of bio-domes and moisture-farms located in the humid mid-canopy, constantly shrouded in steam).

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **Physiology:** Small, hairless humanoids standing 3 to 4 feet tall. Their limbs are long and gangly, adapted for explosive jumping.
* **The Skin:** Their skin is permeable and constantly secretes a mucus layer to stay hydrated. It comes in vibrant "Warning Colors"—electric blues, toxic greens, and crimson reds.
    * *Defense:* Many Ranidae are naturally toxic. Touching a Red Ranidae with bare skin can cause numbness or hallucinations.
* **Adhesion:** Their fingers and toes end in enlarged, sticky pads that allow them to cling to wet glass, metal, or leaves upside down.
* **The Throat Sac:** Males possess a large, inflatable throat sac used for vocalization and, oddly, for storing chemical fumes before exhaling them.

## 2.0 Psychology & Culture
* **The Philosophy of "Dosage":** To a Ranidae, there is no difference between Medicine and Poison; it is merely a question of quantity. They are culturally clinical and detached. They view a plague not as a tragedy, but as a biological equation to be solved.
* **Hygiene:** They are obsessed with purity and contamination. They view dry skin as "dirty." Their greeting rituals involve washing hands in communal bowls of treated water.
* **The Warning Code:** Status is often dictated by color. Brightly colored frogs (highly toxic) are often the warrior/guard caste, while duller green/brown frogs (camouflaged) form the scout and farming castes.

## 3.0 Industrial Role: The Chemical Cartel
* **The Medicine Monopoly:** They produce **"Fever-Bane"** (the only cure for jungle rot) and **"Clot-Moss"** (instant bandages). In a jungle filled with disease, everyone pays the Frog Tax to stay alive.
* **The Black Market:** They brew the recreational and combat drugs for the underworld.
    * **"Viper-Haze":** A smokeable dried frog-slime that slows perception of time.
    * **"Blue-Drift":** A potent narcotic used by the wealthy to sleep through the heat.
* **Pest Control:** They breed the symbiotic insects that eat the wood-boring worms, keeping the city trees alive.

## 4.0 Military Role: The Tox-Troopers
* **Style:** Chemical Warfare / Hit-and-Run.
* **Weaponry:**
    * **Blowguns:** Silent, accurate weapons firing darts tipped with **Neuro-Resin**. One scratch causes paralysis.
    * **Gas Grenades:** Glass spheres filled with pressurized toxic spores.
* **Tactics:** They ignore cover because they can stick to the ceiling. They ambush from above, rain down gas, and leap away before the enemy stops coughing.

## 5.0 Social Quirks
* **The "Taste-Test":** Ranidae often taste things to analyze them. It is not uncommon to see a Frog chemist lightly lick a rock, a plant, or even a coin to determine its composition.
* **Moisture-Suits:** When traveling to dry areas (like the [[Iron Caldera]] or the Upper Canopy), they wear rubberized suits filled with water to keep their skin damp. They look like astronauts in fishbowls.
* **Vocalization:** When thinking or agreeing, they emit a low, rhythmic croak. A room full of agreeing frogs sounds like a swamp at midnight.
