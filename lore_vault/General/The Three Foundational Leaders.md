# The Three Foundational Leaders

After the **Civil War** decimated the ranks of the [[History/The First Wardens|First Wardens]], only three of the original captains remained. Realizing their immortality was broken, they divided the labor of guardianship to ensure the order would survive them.

## 1. The Architect
*Likely Identity: [[Thrum the Mason]]*
* **Role:** Infrastructure and Survival.
* **Achievement:** He constructed **[[The Sacred Spire]]**, a chaos-safe fortress built into the Engineer's torso and head. He designed the deep vaults, plateau farms, and geothermal power systems that sustain the Wardens to this day.

## 2. The Prophet
*Likely Identity: [[Jiro the Resolute]] or [[Liora the Truth-Bearer]]*
* **Role:** Dogma and Recruitment.
* **Achievement:** Developed the religious dogma of the order. He created the **"Divine Imprint"**, a command programed into the evolving tribes of man: *"Guard the prisons"*. This ensures that even without training, the people feel an instinctual drive to protect the Groves.

## 3. The First Kamikaze (The Mage)
*Likely Identity: [[Valen the Witness]]*
* **Role:** Chaos Stabilization and Sacrifice.
* **Achievement:** Conducted bold experiments with chaos energy until his eyes were destroyed, granting him **Chaos Sight** (the ability to see anywhere by tricking chaos).
* **The Sacrifice:** He became the first Warden to sacrifice his life to stabilize a [[Chaos Zones]], establishing the precedent for the "Kamikaze Run" used by the order today.
* **Legacy:** He is the founder of the **Circle of the Eyeless**, the oldest Wardens who burn out their eyes to gain his sight.
