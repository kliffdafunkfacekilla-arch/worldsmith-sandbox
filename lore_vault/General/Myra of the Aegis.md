## The Defection
Myra served as the personal bodyguard to [[Magistar Stagus]]. She watched for centuries as Stagus siphoned the life-force of thousands to maintain the Magistars' immortality. When Stagus ordered the drainage of an entire orphanage to fuel a single spell, Myra turned her shield inward, reflecting the drain back onto the Magistar and breaking the ritual.
