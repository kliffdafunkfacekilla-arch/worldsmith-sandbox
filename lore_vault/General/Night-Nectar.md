# **Night-Nectar**

**Night-Nectar** is a rare, highly restricted alchemical substance harvested from the **Moon-Flower Kin** within the Shatterlands. It is prized for its unique sensory-enhancing and navigational properties.

### **Source & Composition**
- Produced exclusively by the **Moon-Flower Kin**, a sentient botanical variant found in the arid zones and the Dust Bowl.
- These flora utilize **Starlight Synthesis** to produce a dense, glowing, hallucinogenic honey.
- The Moon-Flower Kin operate a non-verbal communication loop known as the *Trinity of Silence* alongside Yucca-Kin and Cactus-Kin.

### **Navigational Properties**
- **Elemental Property:** Aligned with the elemental forces of **Omen (Intuition)**.
- **Foresight:** Ingesting Night-Nectar allows ship **Navigators** to enhance their sensory foresight and temporarily glimpse "probable futures."
- **Application:** Used by elite sky-crews and under-deck pilots to read the "texture of reality" and safely steer Aether-Skiffs through volatile Chaos Zones or tectonic meanders.

### **Legal Status & Contraband Value**
- **Official Status:** Strictly restricted to religious use or sanctioned faction operations.
- **Underworld Trade:** Highly valued as rare contraband by smuggler networks, such as the Gilded Compass and the Canopy Clans' illicit channels. It must be stored in specialized Cactus-Kin reservoirs to maintain its efficacy and prevent physical desiccation.
