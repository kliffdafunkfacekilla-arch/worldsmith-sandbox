# Emperor Kaelos ("The Evasive")

> **"The truth is a blade. You do not hold it by the edge."**

## 1.0 Historical Significance
Kaelos is the cultural saint of **[[The Avian Empire]]**. He is the author of *The Book of Omissions*, the foundational text studied by all Avian lawyers and diplomats on how to navigate the biological inability to lie.

## 2.0 The Legend of the Eye
Every Avian fledgling learns the story of his trial.
* **The Accusation:** The Senate accused him of embezzlement. Kaelos spent six months weaving a masterpiece of technical truths in court, dazzling the public but frustrating the law.
* **The Iron-Beaks:** The High Judge remanded him to the **Order of the Iron-Beak** (Interrogators).
* **The Sacrifice:** It took only one hour to get the truth. Kaelos confessed, but his biological resistance to the "Yes/No" pressure was so violent that he lost an eye in the physical struggle to keep his beak shut.
* **The Artifact:** His lost eye (preserved in amber) sits on the desk of the current High Judge. It serves as the ultimate reminder: **No one is above the Truth.**
