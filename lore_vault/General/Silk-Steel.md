# **Silk-Steel (Iron-Weave)**

**Silk-Steel** (also referred to as **Iron-Weave**) is the highest tensile strength material available in Ostraka, widely utilized for heavy industrial, architectural, and naval construction.

### **Production & Bradypod Monopoly**
- Silk-Steel is harvested from the spinnerets of **Weaver-Cows**, which are actually giant, highly aggressive arachnids (Giant Spiders).
- The harvesting process is the exclusive monopoly of the **Bradypods (Sloth-folk)**. 
- Due to their glacially slow metabolism, 30-second conversational pause, and complete lack of a heat signature, Bradypods move slowly enough to avoid triggering the spiders' predatory vibration-responses. In Ostraka, being slow is an essential industrial requirement.

### **Technical Applications**
- **Naval Rigging & Sails:** Used for the rigging cables and sails of diplomatic Sky-Barques and Aether-Skiffs. Smugglers utilize a variable weave density in Silk-Steel sails to transmit Phase-Chart updates invisibly via surface-light refraction (the *Sail-Cipher*).
- **Structural Anchors:** Crucial for suspension bridges, bridge-cities, and anchoring underground highways in the Shifting Delta against the 49-day tectonic **Meander**. This prevents structural collapse and dampens the catastrophic resonant frequency known as **The Groan**, which would otherwise liquefy tunnel systems and convoys.
- **Underworld Tactical Gear:** The Silk-Sovereign Syndicate utilizes Silk-Steel cables with Bismuth-needle dampeners as high-tension alternatives to iron chains, and wraps their sanctuaries in *Iron-Weave Canvas* to muffle them from external surveillance.

### **Limitations**
- While highly resistant to environmental wear, Silk-Steel is highly sensitive to **Supersonic Kinetic Friction**, which can shear the high-tension cables if not properly shielded.
