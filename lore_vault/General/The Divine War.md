# The Divine War
 
 [[The Exodus]] ritual and the magisters wielding of the power had the effect of catching the eye of the gods.

  **For a generation, the Elder Gods fought directly against the 12 Magistars.** This conflict, known as the **War of the Celestial Scourge**, was not a war of mortal armies, but a clash of absolute cosmic forces. The Gods, weakened by [[The Fae]]'s earlier retreat  were forced to descend from their distant realms to confront the nascent divinity of the Magistars.

- 

- The State of the Conflict: A World Bled Dry

  

  **The War's Toll:** The ongoing war **"bled life and stability from the world."** The ground was scorched not by fire, but by the residue of conflicting cosmic energies. Rivers grew violent currents, and the air was thick  with [[Elara the Pure]], elemental wrath of the gods.

- **The Unholy Fusion:** The Magistars, led by **Kyros**, proved terrifyingly adaptive. They combined their elemental forces in ways [[The Fae]] had warned against and the gods could not counter. **Grom’s** brute Might was fused with **Rictus**’s total perception, allowing their strikes to shatter divine defenses with surgical precision.

- **The Gods' Desperation:** The Elder Gods were fighting to restore the  universal balance. They could not simply destroy the Magistars, as their corrupted power gave them the ability to shield themselves  and even twist the minds of minor gods against there kin.
