# The Titan-Boa (King-Snake)
**Scientific Designation:** *Titanoboa Rex*
**Primary Rancher:** The [[Mungo]] (Mongooses)
**Farm Location:** The Coil-Yards (Suspended cages)

## 1.0 Biology & Appearance
* **Size:** Massive constrictors reaching 40–50 feet in length and weighing over 2 tons.
* **The Scales:** Emerald green with black diamond patterns. As they age, the scales harden into flexible, organic armor that is impervious to water and resistant to slashing.
* **Diet:** Carnivorous. They are fed whole carcasses of "Sump-Pig" or culls from the Terror-Fowl farms once a month.
* **Growth Cycle:** They shed their skin every 3 months. This "Shed" is the primary harvest.

## 2.0 Industrial Utility
* **The Shed-Skin (Low Grade):** Harvested passively. It is translucent and waterproof. Used for tarps, rain-catchers, and cheap windows.
* **The Cull-Hide (High Grade):** When a snake reaches peak size, it is "Culled" (killed). This leather is thick, tough, and beautiful. It is tanned into **Masterwork Leather Armor** and luxury boots for nobility.
* **Bone & Venom:** The massive ribs are used as structural bows for tents. The venom (harvested carefully) is sold to the Frogs for refinement into medical muscle-relaxants.

## 3.0 Temperament & Danger
* **Aggression:** High. They are not domesticated; they are contained. They view everything smaller than them as food.
* **Handling:** Requires "Goad-Teams" of 5-10 Mongooses using electric prods and smell-masking smoke to enter the cage. Accidents are common.
