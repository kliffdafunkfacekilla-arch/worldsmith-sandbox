## The Defection
[[Magistar Opecten]]’s primary surveillance officer. Valen’s job was to watch the world for dissent. He saw a child sharing their last scrap of food with a stranger and realized the Magistars were wrong—humanity was worth saving. He used his awareness to "blind" Opecten's magical sensors, allowing the rebel armies to assemble in secret.
