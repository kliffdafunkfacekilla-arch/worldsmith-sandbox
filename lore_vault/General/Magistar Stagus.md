# Magistar Stagus

> **"The Frozen Eternity"**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School** | [[Ordo]] (Endurance) |
| **Element** | Titanium |
| **Original Title** | The Bulwark |
| **Dragon Form** | [[The Unyielding Glazier]] |
| **Pre-Corruption Name** | Isolde |

## The Rise
Stagus was the anchor of the Magistars' magical core. Her specific talent was the ability to sustain the most demanding rituals without fatigue. She facilitated the **Ritual of the Timeless Core**, absorbing the magical backlash that would have killed the others.

## The Tyranny
Her hubris was the belief that she could outlast Time itself. She became a "black hole" of energy, requiring the siphoned life-force of millions just to maintain her stasis. She froze the social order, ensuring that no citizen could ever rise above their station.

## The Corruption
The chaos froze her soul permanently. She became **The Unyielding Glazier**, a dragon of interlocking Titanium plates. She imposes "Implacable Siege" upon the world, creating storms of absolute zero where nothing ever changes, moves, or lives again.
