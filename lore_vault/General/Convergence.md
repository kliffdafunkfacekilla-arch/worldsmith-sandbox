- **Description** The Convergence is a massive impact crater, widely regarded as the "Ground Zero" of [[The Shattering]]. Within its vast circumference rages a permanent, violent storm of pure chaos, a maelstrom of raw, untamed energy.
- **Significance** This is the central point of the world's chaos, the heart from which much of the planet's instability emanates. It is located perilously close to the resting place of the Slumbering God, the Engineer.
- **Key Features** Suspended within the storm is a floating island, the very site where the Magistars conducted the ritual that broke the world. This location was once the original capital city of the ancient Fae.
- **Significance** The site is a nexus of layered historical tragedies. It was here [[The Fae]] opened their great portal to depart the world, leaving a lingering instability. The Magistars later exploited this weakness, making it the site of their final, world-breaking ritual. It then became the point of impact where they were transformed into dragons. Finally, it was the location of the gods' last gambit, where they sacrificed themselves to imprison the newly formed chaos dragons. It is at this epicenter that the deceptive and corrupting manipulations of [[Sh'lar]], the Prime Corruptor, are at their strongest.
