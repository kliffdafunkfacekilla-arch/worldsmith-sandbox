# Peculiurus (Bank HQ)

**Region:** The Engineer's Mountain Range (Southern Spur) **Role:** Global Financial Hub / The World's Vault **Occupants:** Avian Executives, Hippo Managers, Ostrich Security

> **"It has no windows because money does not need to see the sun. It only needs to be counted."**

## 1.0 Architecture: The Granite Silo

The Spire is not a building; it is a modified mountain peak. The **Roost Building Guild** sheared the outer rock away, leaving a perfect, windowless cylinder of white granite standing 4,000 feet tall.

- **The "Silence" Fields:** The interior is enchanted with permanent sound-dampening geometry. Even in the busy lobby, a shout dies instantly. The only sound is the scratching of quills and the clinking of coins.
    
- **The Atrium (The Teller's Floor):**
    
    - A massive, cathedral-like hall near the summit.
        
    - **The Counter:** A single, continuous slab of black marble running the circumference of the room. Behind it sit the **Hippo Tellers**, unmovable and calm.
        
    - **The Ceiling:** A mirror array that funnels sunlight down from the peak, illuminating the gold on the counter but leaving the corners in shadow.
        

## 2.0 The Deep Vault (The Zero-Point)

Located at the mountain's roots, below the level of the tectonic plate.

- **The Design:** It is a "Floating Room." The vault is suspended by massive magnetic chains within a magma-cooled shaft. If the mountain shakes, the vault remains perfectly still.
    
- **The Gate:** There is no key. The door is a **[[Lex]]-Lock** (Willpower). It opens only when a specific, magically binding truth is spoken by the High Director.
